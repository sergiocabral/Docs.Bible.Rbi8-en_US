[[v19-64-0]]
=== Psalms, 64
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director. A melody of David._

[[v19-64-1]]

^<<v19-64-1,1>>^ Hear, O God, my voice in my concern.
From the dreadfulness of the enemy may you safeguard my life.

[[v19-64-2]]

^<<v19-64-2,2>>^ May you conceal me from the confidential talk of evildoers,
From the tumult of practicers of hurtfulness,

[[v19-64-3]]

^<<v19-64-3,3>>^ Who have sharpened their tongue just like a sword,
Who have aimed their arrow, bitter speech,

[[v19-64-4]]

^<<v19-64-4,4>>^ To shoot from concealed places at someone blameless.
All of a sudden they shoot at him and do not fear.

[[v19-64-5]]

^<<v19-64-5,5>>^ They hold themselves down to bad speech;
They make statements about hiding traps.
They have said: “Who sees them?”^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “Them,” MTLXXVg; Sy, “us.”
====

[[v19-64-6]]

^<<v19-64-6,6>>^ They keep searching out unrighteous things;
They have hidden a shrewd device well searched out,
And the inward part of each one, even &#91;his&#93; heart, is deep.

[[v19-64-7]]

^<<v19-64-7,7>>^ But God will shoot at them with an arrow suddenly.
Wounds have resulted to them,

[[v19-64-8]]

^<<v19-64-8,8>>^ And they cause one to stumble.
&#91;But&#93; their tongue is against their own selves.
All those looking upon them will shake their head,

[[v19-64-9]]

^<<v19-64-9,9>>^ And all earthling men will become afraid;
And they will tell of the activity of God,
And they will certainly have insight into his work.

[[v19-64-10]]

^<<v19-64-10,10>>^ And the righteous one will rejoice in Jehovah and will indeed take refuge in him;
And all the upright in heart will boast.
