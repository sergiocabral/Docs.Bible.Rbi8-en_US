[[v19-89-0]]
=== Psalms, 89
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_Masʹkil.^&#42;1^ Of Eʹthan the Ezʹra·hite._

[[v19-89-1]]

^<<v19-89-1,1>>^ Jehovah’s expressions of loving-kindness^&#42;1^ I will sing about even to time indefinite.
For generation after generation I shall make your faithfulness known with my mouth.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-89-2]]

^<<v19-89-2,2>>^ For I have said: “Loving-kindness will stay built even to time indefinite;
As for the heavens, you keep your faithfulness firmly established in them.”

[[v19-89-3]]

^<<v19-89-3,3>>^ “I have concluded^&#42;1^ a covenant toward my chosen one;
I have sworn to David my servant,

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “cut.”
====

[[v19-89-4]]

^<<v19-89-4,4>>^ ‘Even to time indefinite I shall firmly establish your seed,^&#42;1^
And I will build your throne to generation after generation.’” Seʹlah.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “offspring.”
====

[[v19-89-5]]

^<<v19-89-5,5>>^ And the heavens will laud your marvelous act, O Jehovah,
Yes, your faithfulness in the congregation^&#42;1^ of the holy ones.

[NOTE]
====
. “In the congregation of.” Heb., biq·halʹ; Gr., ek·kle·siʹai; Lat., ec·cleʹsi·a.
====

[[v19-89-6]]

^<<v19-89-6,6>>^ For who in the skies can be compared to Jehovah?
Who can resemble Jehovah among the sons of God?^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “God.” Heb., ʼE·limʹ, pl. of ʼEl, to denote majesty or excellence; Gr., The·ouʹ; Lat., Deʹi; TSy, “angels.” Compare Job 1:6 ftns, “Sons” and “God”; Job 38:7 ftn.
====

[[v19-89-7]]

^<<v19-89-7,7>>^ God^&#42;1^ is to be held in awe among the intimate group of holy ones;
He is grand and fear-inspiring over all who are round about him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “The Divine One.” Heb., ʼEl.
====

[[v19-89-8]]

^<<v19-89-8,8>>^ O Jehovah God of armies,
Who is vigorous like you, O Jah?^&#42;1^
And your faithfulness is all around you.

[NOTE]
====
. See 68:4 ftn.
====

[[v19-89-9]]

^<<v19-89-9,9>>^ You are ruling over the swelling of the sea;
When it raises up its waves you yourself calm them.

[[v19-89-10]]

^<<v19-89-10,10>>^ You yourself have crushed Raʹhab,^&#42;1^ even as someone slain.
By the arm of your strength you have scattered your enemies.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the Stormer.” T, “the arrogant one, that is, impious Pharaoh”; LXXVg, “the arrogant (haughty) one.”
====

[[v19-89-11]]

^<<v19-89-11,11>>^ Heaven is yours, the earth also is yours;
The productive land^&#42;1^ and what fills it—you yourself have founded them.

[NOTE]
====
. “The productive land.” Heb., te·velʹ; LXX, “the inhabited earth”; Lat., orʹbem terʹrae, “the circle of the earth.”
====

[[v19-89-12]]

^<<v19-89-12,12>>^ The north and the south^&#42;1^—you yourself created them;
Taʹbor and Herʹmon—in your name they cry out joyfully.

[NOTE]
====
. “South.” Lit., “right [side],” as one faces east.
====

[[v19-89-13]]

^<<v19-89-13,13>>^ An arm with mightiness is yours,
Your hand is strong,
Your right hand is exalted.

[[v19-89-14]]

^<<v19-89-14,14>>^ Righteousness and judgment are the established place of your throne;
Loving-kindness and trueness^&#42;1^ themselves come in before your face.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “and truthfulness (truth).” Heb., we·ʼemethʹ.
====

[[v19-89-15]]

^<<v19-89-15,15>>^ Happy are the people knowing the joyful shouting.
O Jehovah, in the light of your face they keep walking.

[[v19-89-16]]

^<<v19-89-16,16>>^ In your name they are joyful all day long
And in your righteousness they are exalted.

[[v19-89-17]]

^<<v19-89-17,17>>^ For you are the beauty of their strength;
And by your goodwill our horn is exalted.

[[v19-89-18]]

^<<v19-89-18,18>>^ For our shield belongs to Jehovah,
And our king belongs to the Holy One of Israel.

[[v19-89-19]]

^<<v19-89-19,19>>^ At that time you spoke in a vision to your loyal ones,
And you proceeded to say:
“I have placed help upon a mighty one;
I have exalted a chosen one from among the people.

[[v19-89-20]]

^<<v19-89-20,20>>^ I have found David my servant;
With my holy oil I have anointed him,

[[v19-89-21]]

^<<v19-89-21,21>>^ With whom my own hand will be firm,
Whom my own arm also will strengthen.

[[v19-89-22]]

^<<v19-89-22,22>>^ No enemy will make exactions upon him,
Neither will any son of unrighteousness afflict him.

[[v19-89-23]]

^<<v19-89-23,23>>^ And from before him I crushed his adversaries to pieces,
And to those intensely hating him I kept dealing out blows.

[[v19-89-24]]

^<<v19-89-24,24>>^ And my faithfulness and my loving-kindness are with him,
And in my name his horn is exalted.

[[v19-89-25]]

^<<v19-89-25,25>>^ And on the sea I have put his hand
And on the rivers his right hand.

[[v19-89-26]]

^<<v19-89-26,26>>^ He himself calls out to me, ‘You are my Father,
My God and the Rock of my salvation.’

[[v19-89-27]]

^<<v19-89-27,27>>^ Also, I myself shall place him as firstborn,
The most high of the kings of the earth.

[[v19-89-28]]

^<<v19-89-28,28>>^ To time indefinite I shall preserve my loving-kindness toward him,
And my covenant will be faithful^&#42;1^ to him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “lasting.”
====

[[v19-89-29]]

^<<v19-89-29,29>>^ And I shall certainly set up his seed forever
And his throne as the days of heaven.

[[v19-89-30]]

^<<v19-89-30,30>>^ If his sons leave my law
And in my judicial decisions they do not walk,

[[v19-89-31]]

^<<v19-89-31,31>>^ If they profane my own statutes
And they do not keep my own commandments,

[[v19-89-32]]

^<<v19-89-32,32>>^ I must also turn my attention to their transgression even with a rod
And to their error even with strokes.

[[v19-89-33]]

^<<v19-89-33,33>>^ But my loving-kindness^&#42;1^ I shall not break off from him,
Nor shall I prove false with regard to my faithfulness.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-89-34]]

^<<v19-89-34,34>>^ I shall not profane my covenant,
And the expression out of my lips I shall not change.

[[v19-89-35]]

^<<v19-89-35,35>>^ Once I have sworn in my holiness,
To David I will not tell lies.

[[v19-89-36]]

^<<v19-89-36,36>>^ His seed itself will prove to be even to time indefinite,
And his throne as the sun in front of me.

[[v19-89-37]]

^<<v19-89-37,37>>^ As the moon it will be firmly established for time indefinite,
And &#91;as&#93; a faithful witness in the skies.” Seʹlah.

[[v19-89-38]]

^<<v19-89-38,38>>^ But you—you have cast off and you keep contemning;
You have become furious toward your anointed one.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “Your anointed one.” Heb., meshi·cheʹkha; T(Aram.)Syr., dam·shi·chakh; Gr., khri·stonʹ; Lat., chriʹstum.
====

[[v19-89-39]]

^<<v19-89-39,39>>^ You have spurned the covenant of your servant;
You have profaned his diadem to the very earth.

[[v19-89-40]]

^<<v19-89-40,40>>^ You have broken down all his stone pens;
You have laid his fortifications in ruin.

[[v19-89-41]]

^<<v19-89-41,41>>^ All those passing along the way have pillaged him;
He has become a reproach to his neighbors.

[[v19-89-42]]

^<<v19-89-42,42>>^ You have exalted the right hand of his adversaries;
You have caused all his enemies to rejoice.

[[v19-89-43]]

^<<v19-89-43,43>>^ What is more, you again treat his sword as a foe,
And you have caused him not to gain ground in the battle.

[[v19-89-44]]

^<<v19-89-44,44>>^ You have made &#91;him&#93; cease from his luster,
And his throne you have hurled to the very earth.

[[v19-89-45]]

^<<v19-89-45,45>>^ You have shortened the days of his youth;
You have enwrapped him with shame. Seʹlah.

[[v19-89-46]]

^<<v19-89-46,46>>^ How long, O Jehovah, will you keep yourself concealed? For all time?
Will your rage keep on burning just like a fire?

[[v19-89-47]]

^<<v19-89-47,47>>^ Remember of what duration of life I am.
Is it all in vain that you have created all the sons of men?^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-89-48]]

^<<v19-89-48,48>>^ What able-bodied man is there alive who will not see death?
Can he provide escape for his soul from the hand of Sheʹol? Seʹlah.

[[v19-89-49]]

^<<v19-89-49,49>>^ Where are your former acts of loving-kindness, O Jehovah,^&#42;1^
About which you swore to David in your faithfulness?

[NOTE]
====
. One of 134 scribal changes from YHWH to ʼAdho·naiʹ. See App 1B, 1C §10.
====

[[v19-89-50]]

^<<v19-89-50,50>>^ Remember, O Jehovah,^&#42;1^ the reproach upon your servants,
My carrying in my bosom &#91;the reproach of&#93; all the many peoples,

[NOTE]
====
. See 90:1 ftn.
====

[[v19-89-51]]

^<<v19-89-51,51>>^ How your enemies have reproached, O Jehovah,^&#42;1^
How they have reproached the footprints of your anointed one.

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1C §10.
====

[[v19-89-52]]

^<<v19-89-52,52>>^ Blessed be Jehovah^&#42;1^ to time indefinite. Amen and Amen.

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1C §10.
====
