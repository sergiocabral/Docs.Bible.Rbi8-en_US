[[v19-150-0]]
=== Psalms, 150
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v19-150-1]]

^<<v19-150-1,1>>^ Praise Jah, YOU people!^&#42;1^
Praise God^&#42;2^ in his holy place.
Praise him in the expanse of his strength.

[NOTE]
====
. See 104:35 ftns.
. Lit., “Praise God, you people.” Heb., ha·lelu-ʼElʹ.
====

[[v19-150-2]]

^<<v19-150-2,2>>^ Praise him for his works of mightiness.
Praise him according to the abundance of his greatness.

[[v19-150-3]]

^<<v19-150-3,3>>^ Praise him with the blowing of the horn.^&#42;1^
Praise him with the stringed instrument and the harp.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “shofar.”
====

[[v19-150-4]]

^<<v19-150-4,4>>^ Praise him with the tambourine and the circle dance.
Praise him with strings and the pipe.

[[v19-150-5]]

^<<v19-150-5,5>>^ Praise him with the cymbals of melodious sound.
Praise him with the clashing cymbals.

[[v19-150-6]]

^<<v19-150-6,6>>^ Every breathing thing^&#42;1^—let it praise Jah.^&#42;2^
Praise Jah, YOU people!^&#42;3^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “All the breath.” Compare Ge 2:7.
. “Jah.” Heb., Yah. See 41:13 ftn; 68:4 ftn.
. Thirteen times this 150th Psalm calls for praise to be given to Jehovah.
====
