[[v19-115-0]]
=== Psalms, 115
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v19-115-1]]

^<<v19-115-1,1>>^ ^&#42;1^ To us belongs nothing, O Jehovah, to us belongs nothing,
But to your name give glory
According to your loving-kindness, according to your trueness.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. LXXTh and many Heb. mss combine Ps 114 and 115 to form one continuous psalm (Ps 114) of 26 vss.
. “Your trueness (truthfulness; truth).” Heb., ʼamit·teʹkha; Lat., ve·ri·taʹte tuʹa.
====

[[v19-115-2]]

^<<v19-115-2,2>>^ Why should the nations say:
“Where, now, is their God?”

[[v19-115-3]]

^<<v19-115-3,3>>^ But our God is in the heavens;
Everything that he delighted &#91;to do&#93; he has done.

[[v19-115-4]]

^<<v19-115-4,4>>^ Their idols are silver and gold,
The work of the hands of earthling man.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “Earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-115-5]]

^<<v19-115-5,5>>^ A mouth they have, but they cannot speak;
Eyes they have, but they cannot see;

[[v19-115-6]]

^<<v19-115-6,6>>^ Ears they have, but they cannot hear.
A nose they have, but they cannot smell.

[[v19-115-7]]

^<<v19-115-7,7>>^ Hands are theirs, but they cannot feel.
Feet are theirs, but they cannot walk;
They utter no sound with their throat.

[[v19-115-8]]

^<<v19-115-8,8>>^ Those making them will become just like them,
All those who are trusting in them.

[[v19-115-9]]

^<<v19-115-9,9>>^ O Israel, trust in Jehovah;
He is their help and their shield.

[[v19-115-10]]

^<<v19-115-10,10>>^ O house of Aaron, put YOUR trust in Jehovah;
He is their help and their shield.

[[v19-115-11]]

^<<v19-115-11,11>>^ YOU that fear Jehovah, trust in Jehovah;
He is their help and their shield.

[[v19-115-12]]

^<<v19-115-12,12>>^ Jehovah himself has remembered us; he will bless,
He will bless the house of Israel,
He will bless the house of Aaron.

[[v19-115-13]]

^<<v19-115-13,13>>^ He will bless those fearing Jehovah,
The small ones as well as the great ones.

[[v19-115-14]]

^<<v19-115-14,14>>^ Jehovah will give increase to YOU,
To YOU and to YOUR sons.

[[v19-115-15]]

^<<v19-115-15,15>>^ YOU are the ones blessed by Jehovah,
The Maker of heaven and earth.

[[v19-115-16]]

^<<v19-115-16,16>>^ As regards the heavens, to Jehovah the heavens belong,
But the earth he has given to the sons of men.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-115-17]]

^<<v19-115-17,17>>^ The dead themselves do not praise Jah,^&#42;1^
Nor do any going down into silence.

[NOTE]
====
. “Jah.” Heb., Yah. See 68:4 ftn.
====

[[v19-115-18]]

^<<v19-115-18,18>>^ But we ourselves^&#42;1^ will bless Jah^&#42;2^
From now on and to time indefinite.
Praise Jah, YOU people!^&#42;3^

[NOTE]
====
. “We ourselves,” M; LXX, “we the living”; Vg, “we who are alive.”
. “Jah,” MT; LXXSyVg, “Jehovah.”
. See 104:35 ftns.
====
