[[v19-120-0]]
=== Psalms, 120
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A Song of the Ascents.^&#42;1^_

[[v19-120-1]]

^<<v19-120-1,1>>^ To Jehovah I called in the distress of mine,
And he proceeded to answer me.

[[v19-120-2]]

^<<v19-120-2,2>>^ O Jehovah, do deliver my soul from false lips,
From the tricky tongue.

[[v19-120-3]]

^<<v19-120-3,3>>^ What will one give to you, and what will one add to you,
O you tricky tongue?

[[v19-120-4]]

^<<v19-120-4,4>>^ Sharpened arrows of a mighty man,
Along with burning coals of the broom trees.

[[v19-120-5]]

^<<v19-120-5,5>>^ Woe to me, for I have resided as an alien in Meʹshech!
I have tabernacled together with the tents of Keʹdar.

[[v19-120-6]]

^<<v19-120-6,6>>^ For too long a time my soul has tabernacled
With the haters^&#42;1^ of peace.

[NOTE]
====
. “Haters,” LXXSyVg and eight Heb. mss; M, “hater.”
====

[[v19-120-7]]

^<<v19-120-7,7>>^ I stand for peace; but when I speak,
They are for war.
