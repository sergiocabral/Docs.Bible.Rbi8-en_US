[[v19-47-0]]
=== Psalms, 47
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director. Of the sons of Koʹrah. A melody._

[[v19-47-1]]

^<<v19-47-1,1>>^ All YOU peoples, clap YOUR hands.^&#42;1^
Shout in triumph to God with the sound of a joyful cry.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “palm.”
====

[[v19-47-2]]

^<<v19-47-2,2>>^ For Jehovah, the Most High, is fear-inspiring,
A great King over all the earth.

[[v19-47-3]]

^<<v19-47-3,3>>^ He will subdue peoples under us
And national groups under our feet.

[[v19-47-4]]

^<<v19-47-4,4>>^ He will choose for us our inheritance,
The pride of Jacob, whom he has loved. Seʹlah.

[[v19-47-5]]

^<<v19-47-5,5>>^ God has ascended with joyful shouting,
Jehovah with the sound of the horn.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “shofar.” A ram’s horn used as a trumpet.
====

[[v19-47-6]]

^<<v19-47-6,6>>^ MAKE melody to God, make melody.
Make melody to our King, make melody.

[[v19-47-7]]

^<<v19-47-7,7>>^ For God is King of all the earth;
MAKE melody, acting with discretion.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “Acting with discretion,” by rendering the Heb. participle in the pl. Heb., mas·kilʹ, sing. See 53:2 ftn, “Insight.”
====

[[v19-47-8]]

^<<v19-47-8,8>>^ God has become king over the nations.
God himself has taken his seat upon his holy throne.

[[v19-47-9]]

^<<v19-47-9,9>>^ The nobles of the peoples themselves have gathered together,
&#91;With&#93; the people of the God^&#42;1^ of Abraham.
For to God the shields^&#42;2^ of the earth belong.
He is very high in his ascent.

[NOTE]
====
. “The people of the God,” M; LXXSyVg, “with the God.”
. “Shields,” MT; LXX, “powerful ones”; Sy, “realms (empires)”; Vg, “strong ones.”
====
