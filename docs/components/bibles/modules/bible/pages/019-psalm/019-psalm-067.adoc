[[v19-67-0]]
=== Psalms, 67
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director on stringed instruments. A melody, a song._

[[v19-67-1]]

^<<v19-67-1,1>>^ God himself will show us favor and bless us;
He will make his face shine upon us—Seʹlah—

[[v19-67-2]]

^<<v19-67-2,2>>^ That your way may be known in the earth,
Your salvation even among all the nations.

[[v19-67-3]]

^<<v19-67-3,3>>^ Let peoples laud you, O God;
Let the peoples, all of them, laud you.

[[v19-67-4]]

^<<v19-67-4,4>>^ Let national groups rejoice and cry out joyfully,
For you will judge the peoples with uprightness;
And as for national groups, on the earth you will lead them. Seʹlah.

[[v19-67-5]]

^<<v19-67-5,5>>^ Let peoples laud you, O God;
Let peoples, all of them, laud you.

[[v19-67-6]]

^<<v19-67-6,6>>^ The earth itself will certainly give its produce;
God, our God, will bless us.

[[v19-67-7]]

^<<v19-67-7,7>>^ God will bless us,
And all the ends of the earth will fear him.
