=== Psalms, 131
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A Song of the Ascents. Of David._

[[v19-131-1]]

^<<v19-131-1,1>>^ O Jehovah, my heart has not been haughty,
Nor have my eyes been lofty;
Nor have I walked in things too great,
Nor in things too wonderful for me.

[[v19-131-2]]

^<<v19-131-2,2>>^ Surely I have soothed and quieted my soul
Like a weanling upon his mother.
My soul is like a weanling upon me.

[[v19-131-3]]

^<<v19-131-3,3>>^ Let Israel wait for Jehovah
From now on and to time indefinite.
