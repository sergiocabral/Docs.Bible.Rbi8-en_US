[[v19-101-0]]
=== Psalms, 101
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_Of David. A melody._

[[v19-101-1]]

^<<v19-101-1,1>>^ About loving-kindness and judgment I will sing.
To you, O Jehovah, I will make melody.

[[v19-101-2]]

^<<v19-101-2,2>>^ I will act with discretion in a faultless way.
When will you come to me?
I shall walk about in the integrity of my heart inside my house.

[[v19-101-3]]

^<<v19-101-3,3>>^ I shall not set in front of my eyes any good-for-nothing thing.^&#42;1^
The doing of those who fall away I have hated;
It does not cling to me.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “a thing of belial (worthlessness).”
====

[[v19-101-4]]

^<<v19-101-4,4>>^ A crooked heart departs from me;
Nothing bad do I know.

[[v19-101-5]]

^<<v19-101-5,5>>^ Anyone slandering his companion in secrecy,
Him I silence.
Anyone of haughty eyes and of arrogant heart,^&#42;1^
Him I cannot endure.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “Anyone high of eyes and broad of heart.”
====

[[v19-101-6]]

^<<v19-101-6,6>>^ My eyes are upon the faithful ones of the earth,
That they may dwell with me.
The one walking in a faultless way,
He it is who will minister to me.

[[v19-101-7]]

^<<v19-101-7,7>>^ There will dwell inside my house no worker of trickiness.
As for anyone speaking falsehoods, he will not be firmly established
In front of my eyes.

[[v19-101-8]]

^<<v19-101-8,8>>^ Every morning^&#42;1^ I shall silence all the wicked ones of the earth,
To cut off from the city of Jehovah all the practicers of what is hurtful.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “For the mornings.”
====
