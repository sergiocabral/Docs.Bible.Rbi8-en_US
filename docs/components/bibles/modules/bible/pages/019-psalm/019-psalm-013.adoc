[[v19-13-0]]
=== Psalms, 13
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director. A melody of David._

[[v19-13-1]]

^<<v19-13-1,1>>^ How long, O Jehovah, will you forget me? Forever?
How long will you conceal your face from me?

[[v19-13-2]]

^<<v19-13-2,2>>^ How long shall I set resistance in my soul,
Grief in my heart by day?
How long will my enemy be exalted over me?

[[v19-13-3]]

^<<v19-13-3,3>>^ Do look &#91;upon me&#93;; answer me, O Jehovah my God.
Do make my eyes shine, that I may not fall asleep in death,

[[v19-13-4]]

^<<v19-13-4,4>>^ That my enemy may not say: “I have won out over him!”
&#91;That&#93; my adversaries themselves may &#91;not&#93; be joyful because I am made to stagger.

[[v19-13-5]]

^<<v19-13-5,5>>^ As for me, in your loving-kindness^&#42;1^ I have trusted;
Let my heart be joyful in your salvation.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-13-6]]

^<<v19-13-6,6>>^ I will sing to Jehovah, for he has dealt rewardingly with me.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. LXXVg add: “And I shall make melody to the name of Jehovah the Most High.” See 7:17.
====
