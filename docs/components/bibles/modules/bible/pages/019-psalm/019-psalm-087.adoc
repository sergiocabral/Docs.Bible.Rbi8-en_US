[[v19-87-0]]
=== Psalms, 87
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_Of the sons of Koʹrah. A melody, a song._

[[v19-87-1]]

^<<v19-87-1,1>>^ His foundation is in the holy mountains.

[[v19-87-2]]

^<<v19-87-2,2>>^ Jehovah is more in love with the gates of Zion
Than with all the tabernacles of Jacob.

[[v19-87-3]]

^<<v19-87-3,3>>^ Glorious things are being spoken about you, O city of the &#91;true&#93; God. Seʹlah.

[[v19-87-4]]

^<<v19-87-4,4>>^ I shall make mention of Raʹhab^&#42;1^ and Babylon^&#42;2^ as among those knowing me;
Here are Phi·lisʹti·a and Tyre, together with Cush:^&#42;3^
“This is one who was born there.”

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the Stormer.” T, “the Egyptians.” Compare 89:10 ftn.
. “And Babylon,” LXXVg; Heb., u·Va·velʹ, “and Babel.”
. “Cush.” Heb., Kush; LXXVg, “the Ethiopian people.”
====

[[v19-87-5]]

^<<v19-87-5,5>>^ And respecting Zion it will be said:
“Each and every one^&#42;1^ was born in her.”
And the Most High himself will firmly establish her.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “[This] man and [that] man.”
====

[[v19-87-6]]

^<<v19-87-6,6>>^ Jehovah himself will declare, when recording the peoples:
“This is one who was born there.” Seʹlah.

[[v19-87-7]]

^<<v19-87-7,7>>^ There will also be singers as well as dancers of circle dances:
“All my springs are in you.”
