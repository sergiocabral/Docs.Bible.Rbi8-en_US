[[v19-147-0]]
=== Psalms, 147
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v19-147-1]]

^<<v19-147-1,1>>^ Praise Jah, YOU people,^&#42;1^
For it is good to make melody to our God;
For it is pleasant—praise is fitting.

[NOTE]
====
. See 104:35 ftns.
====

[[v19-147-2]]

^<<v19-147-2,2>>^ Jehovah is building Jerusalem;
The dispersed ones of Israel he brings together.

[[v19-147-3]]

^<<v19-147-3,3>>^ He is healing the brokenhearted ones,
And is binding up their painful spots.

[[v19-147-4]]

^<<v19-147-4,4>>^ He is counting the number of the stars;
All of them he calls by &#91;their&#93; names.

[[v19-147-5]]

^<<v19-147-5,5>>^ Our Lord^&#42;1^ is great and is abundant in power;
His understanding is beyond recounting.

[NOTE]
====
. “Our Lord.” Heb., ʼAdhoh·nehʹnu, pl. of ʼA·dhohnʹ, to denote excellence, and with sing. adjectives, “great” and “abundant.”
====

[[v19-147-6]]

^<<v19-147-6,6>>^ Jehovah is relieving the meek ones;
He is abasing the wicked ones to the earth.

[[v19-147-7]]

^<<v19-147-7,7>>^ Respond to Jehovah with thanksgiving, YOU people;
Make melody to our God on the harp,

[[v19-147-8]]

^<<v19-147-8,8>>^ The One who is covering the heavens with clouds,
The One preparing rain for the earth,
The One making the mountains to sprout green grass.

[[v19-147-9]]

^<<v19-147-9,9>>^ To the beasts he is giving their food,
To the young ravens^&#42;1^ that keep calling.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “sons of the raven.”
====

[[v19-147-10]]

^<<v19-147-10,10>>^ Not in the mightiness of the horse does he take delight,
Nor in the legs of the man^&#42;1^ does he find pleasure.

[NOTE]
====
. “The man.” Heb., ha·ʼishʹ.
====

[[v19-147-11]]

^<<v19-147-11,11>>^ Jehovah is finding pleasure in those fearing him,
In those waiting for his loving-kindness.

[[v19-147-12]]

^<<v19-147-12,12>>^ Commend Jehovah, O Jerusalem.
Praise your God, O Zion.

[[v19-147-13]]

^<<v19-147-13,13>>^ For he has made the bars of your gates strong;
He has blessed your sons in the midst of you.

[[v19-147-14]]

^<<v19-147-14,14>>^ He is putting peace in your territory;
With the fat^&#42;1^ of the wheat he keeps satisfying you.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the best part.”
====

[[v19-147-15]]

^<<v19-147-15,15>>^ He is sending his saying to the earth;
With speed his word runs.

[[v19-147-16]]

^<<v19-147-16,16>>^ He is giving snow like wool;
Hoarfrost he scatters just like ashes.

[[v19-147-17]]

^<<v19-147-17,17>>^ He is throwing his ice like morsels.
Before his cold who can stand?

[[v19-147-18]]

^<<v19-147-18,18>>^ He sends forth his word and melts them.
He causes his wind^&#42;1^ to blow;
The waters trickle.

[NOTE]
====
. “His wind.” Heb., ru·chohʹ; Gr., pneuʹma; Lat., spiʹri·tus. Compare 146:4 ftn, “Spirit”; Ge 1:2 ftn, “Force.”
====

[[v19-147-19]]

^<<v19-147-19,19>>^ He is telling his word to Jacob,
His regulations and his judicial decisions to Israel.

[[v19-147-20]]

^<<v19-147-20,20>>^ He has not done that way to any other nation;
And as for &#91;his&#93; judicial decisions, they have not known them.^&#42;1^
Praise Jah, YOU people!

[NOTE]
====
. “And his judicial decisions he has not made known to them,” TLXXSyVg.
====
