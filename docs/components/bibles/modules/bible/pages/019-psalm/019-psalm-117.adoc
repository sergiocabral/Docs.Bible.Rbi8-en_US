=== Psalms, 117
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v19-117-1]]

^<<v19-117-1,1>>^ Praise Jehovah, all YOU nations;
Commend him, all YOU clans.

[[v19-117-2]]

^<<v19-117-2,2>>^ For toward us his loving-kindness^&#42;1^ has proved mighty;
And the trueness of Jehovah^&#42;2^ is to time indefinite.
Praise Jah, YOU people!

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
. Or, “and the truthfulness (truth) of Jehovah.” Heb., we·ʼemeth-Yehwahʹ.
====
