[[v19-130-0]]
=== Psalms, 130
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-130-1]]

^<<v19-130-1,1>>^ Out of the depths I have called upon you, O Jehovah.

[[v19-130-2]]

^<<v19-130-2,2>>^ O Jehovah,^&#42;1^ do hear my voice.
May your ears prove to be attentive to the voice of my entreaties.

[NOTE]
====
. One of 134 scribal changes from YHWH to ʼAdho·naiʹ. See App 1B.
====

[[v19-130-3]]

^<<v19-130-3,3>>^ If errors were what you watch, O Jah,^&#42;1^
O Jehovah,^&#42;2^ who could stand?

[NOTE]
====
. “Jah.” Heb. and T(Aram.), Yah; LXXVg, “Jehovah.” See 68:4 ftn.
. See vs 2 ftn.
====

[[v19-130-4]]

^<<v19-130-4,4>>^ For there is the &#91;true&#93; forgiveness with you,
In order that you may be feared.

[[v19-130-5]]

^<<v19-130-5,5>>^ I have hoped, O Jehovah, my soul has hoped,
And for his word I have waited.

[[v19-130-6]]

^<<v19-130-6,6>>^ My soul &#91;has waited&#93; for Jehovah^&#42;1^
More than watchmen for the morning,
Watching for the morning.

[NOTE]
====
. See vs 2 ftn.
====

[[v19-130-7]]

^<<v19-130-7,7>>^ Let Israel keep waiting for Jehovah.
For there is loving-kindness^&#42;1^ with Jehovah,
And abundantly so is there redemption with him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-130-8]]

^<<v19-130-8,8>>^ And he himself will redeem Israel out of all his errors.
