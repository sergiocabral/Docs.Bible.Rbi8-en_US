[[v19-107-0]]
=== Psalms, 107
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_BOOK FIVE_

_(Psalms 107 – 150)_

[[v19-107-1]]

^<<v19-107-1,1>>^ O give thanks to Jehovah, YOU people, for he is good;
For his loving-kindness^&#42;1^ is to time indefinite.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-107-2]]

^<<v19-107-2,2>>^ Let the reclaimed ones of Jehovah say so,
Whom he has reclaimed from the hand of the adversary,

[[v19-107-3]]

^<<v19-107-3,3>>^ And whom he has collected together even from the lands,
From the sunrise and from the sunset,
From the north and from the south.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “the sea,” that is, the Red Sea to the south.
====

[[v19-107-4]]

^<<v19-107-4,4>>^ They wandered about in the wilderness, in the desert;
They did not find any way to a city of habitation.

[[v19-107-5]]

^<<v19-107-5,5>>^ They were hungry, also thirsty;
Their very soul within them began to faint away.

[[v19-107-6]]

^<<v19-107-6,6>>^ And they kept crying out to Jehovah in their distress;
Out of the stresses upon them he proceeded to deliver them,

[[v19-107-7]]

^<<v19-107-7,7>>^ And to have them walk in the right way,
So as to come to a city of habitation.

[[v19-107-8]]

^<<v19-107-8,8>>^ O let people give thanks to Jehovah for his loving-kindness
And for his wonderful works to the sons of men.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-107-9]]

^<<v19-107-9,9>>^ For he has satisfied the dried-out soul;
And the hungry soul he has filled with good things.

[[v19-107-10]]

^<<v19-107-10,10>>^ There were those dwelling in darkness and deep shadow,
Prisoners in affliction and irons.

[[v19-107-11]]

^<<v19-107-11,11>>^ For they had behaved rebelliously against the sayings of God;^&#42;1^
And the counsel of the Most High they had disrespected.

[NOTE]
====
. “The sayings of God.” Heb., ʼim·reh-ʼElʹ.
====

[[v19-107-12]]

^<<v19-107-12,12>>^ So with trouble he proceeded to subdue their heart;
They stumbled, and there was no one helping.

[[v19-107-13]]

^<<v19-107-13,13>>^ And they began calling to Jehovah for help in their distress;
Out of the stresses upon them he as usual saved them.

[[v19-107-14]]

^<<v19-107-14,14>>^ He went bringing them out from darkness and deep shadow,
And tearing even their bands apart.

[[v19-107-15]]

^<<v19-107-15,15>>^ O let people give thanks to Jehovah for his loving-kindness
And for his wonderful works to the sons of men.

[[v19-107-16]]

^<<v19-107-16,16>>^ For he has broken the doors of copper,
And he has cut down even the bars of iron.

[[v19-107-17]]

^<<v19-107-17,17>>^ Those who were foolish, due to the way of their transgression
And due to their errors, finally caused themselves affliction.

[[v19-107-18]]

^<<v19-107-18,18>>^ Their soul got to detest even every sort of food,
And they were arriving at the gates of death.

[[v19-107-19]]

^<<v19-107-19,19>>^ And they began calling to Jehovah for help in their distress;
Out of the stresses upon them he as usual saved them.

[[v19-107-20]]

^<<v19-107-20,20>>^ He proceeded to send his word and heal them
And to provide &#91;them&#93; escape out of their pits.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Possibly, “the pit for their lives.”
====

[[v19-107-21]]

^<<v19-107-21,21>>^ O let people give thanks to Jehovah for his loving-kindness
And for his wonderful works to the sons of men.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. See vs 8 ftn.
====

[[v19-107-22]]

^<<v19-107-22,22>>^ And let them offer^&#42;1^ the sacrifices of thanksgiving
And declare his works with a joyful cry.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “sacrifice.”
====

[[v19-107-23]]

^<<v19-107-23,23>>^ Those going down to the sea in the ships,
Doing business on vast^&#42;1^ waters,

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “many.”
====

[[v19-107-24]]

^<<v19-107-24,24>>^ They are the ones that have seen the works of Jehovah
And his wonderful works in the depths;

[[v19-107-25]]

^<<v19-107-25,25>>^ How he says &#91;the word&#93; and causes a tempestuous wind^&#42;1^ to arise,
So that it lifts up its waves.

[NOTE]
====
. “Wind.” Heb., ruʹach. Compare Ge 1:2 ftn, “Force.”
====

[[v19-107-26]]

^<<v19-107-26,26>>^ They go up to the heavens,
They go down to the bottoms.^&#42;1^
Because of the calamity their very soul finds itself melting.

[NOTE]
====
. “Bottoms.” Heb., thehoh·mohthʹ; LXXVg, “abysses.”
====

[[v19-107-27]]

^<<v19-107-27,27>>^ They reel and move unsteadily like a drunken man,
And even all their wisdom proves confused.

[[v19-107-28]]

^<<v19-107-28,28>>^ And they begin crying out to Jehovah in their distress,
And out of the stresses upon them he brings them forth.

[[v19-107-29]]

^<<v19-107-29,29>>^ He causes the windstorm to stand at a calm,
So that the waves of the sea^&#42;1^ keep quiet.

[NOTE]
====
. “The waves of the sea,” Sy; MT, “their waves”; LXXVg, “its waves.”
====

[[v19-107-30]]

^<<v19-107-30,30>>^ And they rejoice because these become still,
And he leads them to the haven of their delight.

[[v19-107-31]]

^<<v19-107-31,31>>^ O let people give thanks to Jehovah for his loving-kindness
And for his wonderful works to the sons of men.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. See vs 8 ftn.
====

[[v19-107-32]]

^<<v19-107-32,32>>^ And let them extol him in the congregation^&#42;1^ of the people;
And in the seat of the elderly men let them praise him.

[NOTE]
====
. “In the congregation of.” Heb., biq·halʹ; Gr., ek·kle·siʹai; Lat., ec·cleʹsi·a.
====

[[v19-107-33]]

^<<v19-107-33,33>>^ He converts rivers into a wilderness,
And the outflows of water into thirsty ground,

[[v19-107-34]]

^<<v19-107-34,34>>^ Fruitful land into salt country,
Owing to the badness of those dwelling in it.

[[v19-107-35]]

^<<v19-107-35,35>>^ He converts a wilderness into a reedy pool of water,
And the land of a waterless region into outflows of water.

[[v19-107-36]]

^<<v19-107-36,36>>^ And there he causes the hungry ones to dwell,
So that they firmly establish a city of habitation.

[[v19-107-37]]

^<<v19-107-37,37>>^ And they sow fields and plant vineyards,
That they may yield fruitful crops.

[[v19-107-38]]

^<<v19-107-38,38>>^ And he blesses them so that they become very many;
And he does not let their cattle become few.

[[v19-107-39]]

^<<v19-107-39,39>>^ Again they become few and crouch down
Owing to restraint, calamity and grief.

[[v19-107-40]]

^<<v19-107-40,40>>^ He is pouring out contempt upon nobles,
So that he makes them wander about in a featureless place, where there is no way.

[[v19-107-41]]

^<<v19-107-41,41>>^ But he protects^&#42;1^ the poor one from affliction
And converts him into families just like a flock.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “puts on high,” that is, out of reach.
====

[[v19-107-42]]

^<<v19-107-42,42>>^ The upright ones see and rejoice;
But as for all unrighteousness, it has to shut its mouth.

[[v19-107-43]]

^<<v19-107-43,43>>^ Who is wise? He will both observe these things
And show himself attentive toward Jehovah’s acts of loving-kindness.
