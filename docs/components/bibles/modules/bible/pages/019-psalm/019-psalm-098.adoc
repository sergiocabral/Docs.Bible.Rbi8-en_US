[[v19-98-0]]
=== Psalms, 98
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A melody.^&#42;1^_

[[v19-98-1]]

^<<v19-98-1,1>>^ SING to Jehovah a new song,
For wonderful are the things that he has done.
His right hand, even his holy arm, has gained salvation^&#42;1^ for him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “victory.”
====

[[v19-98-2]]

^<<v19-98-2,2>>^ Jehovah has made his salvation known;
In the eyes of the nations he has revealed his righteousness.

[[v19-98-3]]

^<<v19-98-3,3>>^ He has remembered his loving-kindness^&#42;1^ and his faithfulness to the house of Israel.
All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation by our God.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-98-4]]

^<<v19-98-4,4>>^ Shout in triumph to Jehovah, all &#91;YOU people of&#93; the earth.^&#42;1^
Be cheerful and cry out joyfully and make melody.

[NOTE]
====
. See 96:1 ftn, “Earth.”
====

[[v19-98-5]]

^<<v19-98-5,5>>^ Make melody to Jehovah with the harp,
With the harp and the voice of melody.

[[v19-98-6]]

^<<v19-98-6,6>>^ With the trumpets^&#42;1^ and the sound of the horn^&#42;2^
Shout in triumph before the King, Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. That is, straight trumpets. See Nu 10:2 ftn.
. Or, “shofar,” an animal’s curved horn.
====

[[v19-98-7]]

^<<v19-98-7,7>>^ Let the sea thunder and that which fills it,
The productive land^&#42;1^ and those dwelling in it.

[NOTE]
====
. “The productive land.” Heb., te·velʹ; LXX, “the inhabited earth”; Lat., orʹbis ter·raʹrum, “the circle of the whole earth.”
====

[[v19-98-8]]

^<<v19-98-8,8>>^ Let the rivers themselves clap their hands;^&#42;1^
All together let the very mountains cry out joyfully

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “clap the palm.”
====

[[v19-98-9]]

^<<v19-98-9,9>>^ Before Jehovah, for he has come to judge the earth.
He will judge the productive land with righteousness
And the peoples with uprightness.
