[[v19-128-0]]
=== Psalms, 128
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-128-1]]

^<<v19-128-1,1>>^ Happy is everyone fearing Jehovah,
Who is walking in his ways.

[[v19-128-2]]

^<<v19-128-2,2>>^ For you will eat the toil of your own hands.
Happy you will be and it will be well with you.

[[v19-128-3]]

^<<v19-128-3,3>>^ Your wife will be like a fruit-bearing vine
In the innermost parts of your house.
Your sons will be like slips of olive trees all around your table.

[[v19-128-4]]

^<<v19-128-4,4>>^ Look! That is how the able-bodied man will be blessed
Who fears Jehovah.

[[v19-128-5]]

^<<v19-128-5,5>>^ Jehovah will bless you out of Zion.
See also^&#42;1^ the good of Jerusalem all the days of your life,

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “So see.”
====

[[v19-128-6]]

^<<v19-128-6,6>>^ And see the sons of your sons.
May there be peace upon Israel.
