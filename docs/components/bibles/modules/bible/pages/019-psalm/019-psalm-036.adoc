[[v19-36-0]]
=== Psalms, 36
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director. Of Jehovah’s^&#42;1^ servant, David._

[[v19-36-1]]

^<<v19-36-1,1>>^ The utterance of transgression to the wicked one is in the midst of his^&#42;1^ heart;
There is no dread of God in front of his eyes.

[NOTE]
====
. “His,” LXXSyVg and a few Heb. mss; MT, “my.”
====

[[v19-36-2]]

^<<v19-36-2,2>>^ For he has acted too smoothly to himself in his own eyes
To find out his error so as to hate &#91;it&#93;.

[[v19-36-3]]

^<<v19-36-3,3>>^ The words of his mouth are hurtfulness and deception;
He has ceased to have insight for doing good.

[[v19-36-4]]

^<<v19-36-4,4>>^ Hurtfulness is what he keeps scheming upon his bed.
He stations himself on a way that is not good.
What is bad he does not reject.

[[v19-36-5]]

^<<v19-36-5,5>>^ O Jehovah,^&#42;1^ your loving-kindness^&#42;2^ is in the heavens;
Your faithfulness is up to the clouds.

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1C §10.
. Or, “loyal love.”
====

[[v19-36-6]]

^<<v19-36-6,6>>^ Your righteousness is like mountains of God;^&#42;1^
Your judicial decision is a vast watery deep.^&#42;2^
Man^&#42;3^ and beast you save, O Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. “God.” Heb., ʼEl.
. Or, “vast body of surging waters.” Heb., tehohmʹ rab·bahʹ. See 33:7 ftn, “Waters”; Ge 1:2 and Ge 7:11 ftns, “Deep.”
. Or, “Mankind.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-36-7]]

^<<v19-36-7,7>>^ How precious your loving-kindness is, O God!^&#42;1^
And in the shadow of your wings the sons of men themselves take refuge.

[NOTE]
====
. “God.” Heb., ʼElo·himʹ.
====

[[v19-36-8]]

^<<v19-36-8,8>>^ They drink their fill of the fatness of your house;
And of the torrent of your pleasures you cause them to drink.

[[v19-36-9]]

^<<v19-36-9,9>>^ For with you is the source^&#42;1^ of life;
By light from you we can see light.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “well.” Heb., meqohrʹ.
====

[[v19-36-10]]

^<<v19-36-10,10>>^ Continue your loving-kindness to those knowing you,
And your righteousness to those upright in heart.

[[v19-36-11]]

^<<v19-36-11,11>>^ O may not the foot of haughtiness come &#91;against&#93; me;
As for the hand of wicked people, let it not make me a wanderer.

[[v19-36-12]]

^<<v19-36-12,12>>^ There the practicers of hurtfulness have fallen;
They have been pushed down and have been unable to get up.
