[[v19-55-0]]
=== Psalms, 55
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_To the director on stringed instruments. Masʹkil.^&#42;1^ Of David._

[[v19-55-1]]

^<<v19-55-1,1>>^ Do give ear, O God, to my prayer;
And do not hide yourself from my request for favor.

[[v19-55-2]]

^<<v19-55-2,2>>^ Do pay attention to me and answer me.
I am driven restlessly about by my concern,
And I cannot but show disquietude,

[[v19-55-3]]

^<<v19-55-3,3>>^ Due to the voice of the enemy, because of the pressure of the wicked one.
For they keep dropping upon me what is hurtful,
And in anger they harbor animosity against me.

[[v19-55-4]]

^<<v19-55-4,4>>^ My very heart is in severe pain within me,
And the frights of death itself have fallen upon me.

[[v19-55-5]]

^<<v19-55-5,5>>^ Fear, yes, trembling itself enters into me,
And shuddering covers me.

[[v19-55-6]]

^<<v19-55-6,6>>^ And I keep saying: “O that I had wings as a dove has!
I would fly away and reside.

[[v19-55-7]]

^<<v19-55-7,7>>^ Look! I would go far away in flight;
I would lodge in the wilderness.—Seʹlah—

[[v19-55-8]]

^<<v19-55-8,8>>^ I would hasten to a place of escape for me
From the rushing^&#42;1^ wind, from the tempest.”

[NOTE]
====
. “Calumniating,” according to another derivation of the participle.
====

[[v19-55-9]]

^<<v19-55-9,9>>^ Confuse, O Jehovah,^&#42;1^ divide their tongue,
For I have seen violence and disputing in the city.

[NOTE]
====
. One of 134 scribal changes from YHWH to ʼAdho·naiʹ. See App 1B.
====

[[v19-55-10]]

^<<v19-55-10,10>>^ Day and night they go round about it upon its walls;
And hurtfulness and trouble are within it.

[[v19-55-11]]

^<<v19-55-11,11>>^ Adversities are within it;
And from its public square oppression and deception have not moved away.

[[v19-55-12]]

^<<v19-55-12,12>>^ For it was not an enemy that proceeded to reproach me;
Otherwise I could put up with it.
It was not an intense hater of me that assumed great airs against me;
Otherwise I could conceal myself from him.

[[v19-55-13]]

^<<v19-55-13,13>>^ But it was you, a mortal man who was as my equal,
One familiar to me and my acquaintance,

[[v19-55-14]]

^<<v19-55-14,14>>^ Because we used to enjoy sweet intimacy together;
Into the house of God we used to walk with the throng.

[[v19-55-15]]

^<<v19-55-15,15>>^ Desolations &#91;be&#93; upon them!
Let them go down into Sheʹol alive;
For during their alien residence bad things have been within them.

[[v19-55-16]]

^<<v19-55-16,16>>^ As for me, to God I shall call out;
And Jehovah himself will save me.

[[v19-55-17]]

^<<v19-55-17,17>>^ Evening and morning and noontime I cannot but show concern and I moan,
And he hears my voice.

[[v19-55-18]]

^<<v19-55-18,18>>^ He will certainly redeem &#91;and put&#93; my soul in peace from the fight that is against me,
For in multitudes they have come to be against me.

[[v19-55-19]]

^<<v19-55-19,19>>^ God^&#42;1^ will hear and answer them,
Even He that is sitting &#91;enthroned&#93; as in the past—Seʹlah—
Those with whom there are no changings^&#42;2^
And who have not feared God.

[NOTE]
====
. “God.” Heb., ʼEl.
. Or, “vicissitudes”; or, “mutual liabilities.”
====

[[v19-55-20]]

^<<v19-55-20,20>>^ He has thrust out his hands against those at peace with him;
He has profaned his covenant.

[[v19-55-21]]

^<<v19-55-21,21>>^ Smoother than butter^&#42;1^ are &#91;the words of&#93; his mouth,
But his heart is disposed to fight.^&#42;2^
His words are softer than oil,
But they are drawn swords.

[NOTE]
====
. By a correction of M.
. Lit., “is fight (war).”
====

[[v19-55-22]]

^<<v19-55-22,22>>^ Throw your burden^&#42;1^ upon Jehovah himself,
And he himself will sustain you.
Never will he allow the righteous one to totter.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “lot” (that which is given).
====

[[v19-55-23]]

^<<v19-55-23,23>>^ But you yourself, O God, will bring them down to the lowest pit.
As for bloodguilty and deceitful men,^&#42;1^ they will not live out half^&#42;2^ their days.
But as for me, I shall trust in you.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “men of bloods and deception.”
. Lit., “will not halve.”
====
