[[v19-116-0]]
=== Psalms, 116
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v19-116-1]]

^<<v19-116-1,1>>^ I do love, because Jehovah hears^&#42;1^
My voice, my entreaties.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “I have loved that Jehovah should hear.”
. “My voice, my entreaties,” M; LXXSyVg, “the voice of my supplication (prayer).”
====

[[v19-116-2]]

^<<v19-116-2,2>>^ For he has inclined his ear to me,
And throughout my days I shall call.

[[v19-116-3]]

^<<v19-116-3,3>>^ The ropes of death encircled me
And the distressing circumstances of Sheʹol themselves found me.
Distress and grief I kept finding.

[[v19-116-4]]

^<<v19-116-4,4>>^ But upon the name of Jehovah I proceeded to call:
“Ah, Jehovah, do provide my soul with escape!”

[[v19-116-5]]

^<<v19-116-5,5>>^ Jehovah is gracious and righteous;
And our God is One showing mercy.

[[v19-116-6]]

^<<v19-116-6,6>>^ Jehovah is guarding the inexperienced ones.
I was impoverished, and he proceeded to save even me.

[[v19-116-7]]

^<<v19-116-7,7>>^ Return to your resting-place, O my soul,
For Jehovah himself has acted appropriately toward you.

[[v19-116-8]]

^<<v19-116-8,8>>^ For you have rescued my soul from death,
My eye from tears, my foot from stumbling.

[[v19-116-9]]

^<<v19-116-9,9>>^ I will walk before Jehovah in the lands of those living.

[[v19-116-10]]

^<<v19-116-10,10>>^ ^&#42;1^ I had faith, for I proceeded to speak.
I myself was very much afflicted.

[NOTE]
====
. LXXVg begin their Ps 115 here with the superscription, “Hallelujah.”
====

[[v19-116-11]]

^<<v19-116-11,11>>^ I, for my part, said, when I became panicky:
“Every man^&#42;1^ is a liar.”

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “All mankind.” Heb., kol-ha·ʼa·dhamʹ.
====

[[v19-116-12]]

^<<v19-116-12,12>>^ What shall I repay to Jehovah
For all his benefits to me?

[[v19-116-13]]

^<<v19-116-13,13>>^ The cup of grand salvation I shall take up,
And on the name of Jehovah I shall call.

[[v19-116-14]]

^<<v19-116-14,14>>^ My vows I shall pay to Jehovah,
Yes, in front of all his people.

[[v19-116-15]]

^<<v19-116-15,15>>^ Precious in the eyes of Jehovah
Is the death of his loyal ones.

[[v19-116-16]]

^<<v19-116-16,16>>^ Ah, now, O Jehovah,
For I am your servant.
I am your servant, the son of your slave girl.
You have loosened my bands.

[[v19-116-17]]

^<<v19-116-17,17>>^ To you I shall offer the sacrifice of thanksgiving,
And on the name of Jehovah I shall call.

[[v19-116-18]]

^<<v19-116-18,18>>^ My vows I shall pay to Jehovah,
Yes, in front of all his people,

[[v19-116-19]]

^<<v19-116-19,19>>^ In the courtyards of the house of Jehovah,
In the midst of you, O Jerusalem.
Praise Jah, YOU people!^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. See 104:35 ftns.
====
