[[v19-84-0]]
=== Psalms, 84
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_For the director upon the Gitʹtith.^&#42;1^ Of the sons of Koʹrah. A melody._

[[v19-84-1]]

^<<v19-84-1,1>>^ How lovely your grand tabernacle is,
O Jehovah of armies!

[[v19-84-2]]

^<<v19-84-2,2>>^ My soul has yearned and also pined away for the courtyards of Jehovah.
My own heart and my very flesh cry out joyfully to the living God.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the living Divine One.” Heb., ʼEl-chaiʹ.
====

[[v19-84-3]]

^<<v19-84-3,3>>^ Even the bird itself has found a house,
And the swallow a nest for herself,
Where she has put her young ones—
Your grand altar,^&#42;1^ O Jehovah of armies, my King and my God!^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “your altars,” pl., likely to denote grandeur. Compare “grand tabernacle” in vs 1.
. “And my God.” Heb., wEʼ·lo·haiʹ.
====

[[v19-84-4]]

^<<v19-84-4,4>>^ Happy are those dwelling in your house!
They still keep on praising you. Seʹlah.

[[v19-84-5]]

^<<v19-84-5,5>>^ Happy are the men^&#42;1^ whose strength is in you,^&#42;2^
In whose heart are the highways.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
. “You,” MVg; LXX, “you, O Jehovah”; T, “your Meimra.”
====

[[v19-84-6]]

^<<v19-84-6,6>>^ Passing along through the low plain of the baʹca bushes,
They turn it into a spring itself;
Even with blessings the instructor enwraps himself.

[[v19-84-7]]

^<<v19-84-7,7>>^ They will walk on from vital energy to vital energy;
Each one appears to God in Zion.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “They will see the God of gods in Zion,” by a slight correction in M; LXXSyVg, “the God of gods will be seen in Zion.”
====

[[v19-84-8]]

^<<v19-84-8,8>>^ O Jehovah God^&#42;1^ of armies, do hear my prayer;
Do give ear, O God of Jacob. Seʹlah.

[NOTE]
====
. “God of.” Heb., ʼElo·himʹ.
====

[[v19-84-9]]

^<<v19-84-9,9>>^ O shield of ours, see, O God,
And look upon the face of your anointed one.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “your messiah.” Heb., meshi·cheʹkha; Gr., khri·stouʹ; Syr., meshi·chakh; Lat., chriʹsti.
====

[[v19-84-10]]

^<<v19-84-10,10>>^ For a day in your courtyards is better than a thousand &#91;elsewhere&#93;.
I have chosen to stand at the threshold in the house of my God
Rather than to move around in the tents of wickedness.

[[v19-84-11]]

^<<v19-84-11,11>>^ For Jehovah God is a sun and a shield;
Favor and glory are what he gives.
Jehovah himself will not hold back anything good from those walking in faultlessness.

[[v19-84-12]]

^<<v19-84-12,12>>^ O Jehovah of armies, happy is the man^&#42;1^ that is trusting in you.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====
