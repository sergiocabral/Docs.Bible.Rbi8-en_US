// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_א &#91;ʼAʹleph&#93;^&#42;1^_

[[v19-119-1]]

^<<v19-119-1,1>>^ ^&#42;1^ Happy are the ones faultless in &#91;their&#93; way,
The ones walking in the law of Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. This is an acrostic, or alphabetic, psalm with 22 sections to correspond to the 22 letters of the Heb. alphabet; each section contains eight vss.
====

[[v19-119-2]]

^<<v19-119-2,2>>^ Happy are those observing his reminders;
With all the heart they keep searching for him.

[[v19-119-3]]

^<<v19-119-3,3>>^ Really they have practiced no unrighteousness.
In his ways they have walked.

[[v19-119-4]]

^<<v19-119-4,4>>^ You yourself have commandingly given your orders
To be carefully kept.

[[v19-119-5]]

^<<v19-119-5,5>>^ O that my ways were firmly established
To keep your regulations!

[[v19-119-6]]

^<<v19-119-6,6>>^ In that case I should not be ashamed,
When I look to all your commandments.

[[v19-119-7]]

^<<v19-119-7,7>>^ I shall laud you in uprightness of heart,
When I learn your righteous judicial decisions.

[[v19-119-8]]

^<<v19-119-8,8>>^ Your regulations I continue to keep.
O do not leave me entirely.

_ב &#91;Behth&#93;^&#42;1^_

[[v19-119-9]]

Do not cause me to go astray from your commandments.

[[v19-119-11]]

^<<v19-119-11,11>>^ In my heart I have treasured up^&#42;1^ your saying,
In order that I may not sin against you.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “I have concealed.”
====
