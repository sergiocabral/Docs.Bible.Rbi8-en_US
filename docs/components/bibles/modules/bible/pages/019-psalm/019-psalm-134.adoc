=== Psalms, 134
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

_A Song of the Ascents.^&#42;1^_

[[v19-134-1]]

^<<v19-134-1,1>>^ O bless Jehovah,
All YOU servants of Jehovah,
YOU who are standing in the house of Jehovah during the nights.

[[v19-134-2]]

^<<v19-134-2,2>>^ Raise YOUR hands in holiness
And bless Jehovah.

[[v19-134-3]]

^<<v19-134-3,3>>^ May Jehovah bless you^&#42;1^ out of Zion,
He the Maker of heaven and earth.

[NOTE]
====
. “You,” sing.
====
