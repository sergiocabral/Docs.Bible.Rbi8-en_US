[[v23-54-0]]
=== Isaiah, 54
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v23-54-1]]

^<<v23-54-1,1>>^ “Cry out joyfully, you barren woman that did not give birth! Become cheerful with a joyful outcry and cry shrilly, you that had no childbirth pains, for the sons of the desolated one are more numerous than the sons of the woman with a husbandly owner,” Jehovah has said.

[[v23-54-2]]

^<<v23-54-2,2>>^ “Make the place of your tent more spacious. And let them stretch out^&#42;1^ the tent cloths of your grand tabernacle.^&#42;2^ Do not hold back. Lengthen out your tent cords, and make those tent pins of yours strong.

[NOTE]
====
. “Let them stretch out,” M; 1QIsa, “let her stretch out”; LXXSyVg, “stretch out.”
. “Grand tabernacle.” Lit., “tabernacles,” pl., evidently to denote grandeur.
====

[[v23-54-3]]

^<<v23-54-3,3>>^ For to the right and to the left you will break forth, and your own offspring^&#42;1^ will take possession even of nations, and they will inhabit even the desolated cities.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “seed.”
====

[[v23-54-4]]

^<<v23-54-4,4>>^ Do not be afraid, for you will not be put to shame; and do not feel humiliated, for you will not be disappointed. For you will forget even the shame of your time of youth, and the reproach of your continuous widowhood^&#42;1^ you will remember no more.”

[NOTE]
====
. “Your continuous widowhood.” Lit., “your widowhoods,” pl. to show prolongation.
====

[[v23-54-5]]

^<<v23-54-5,5>>^ “For your Grand Maker^&#42;1^ is your husbandly owner,^&#42;2^ Jehovah of armies being his name; and the Holy One of Israel is your Repurchaser. The God of the whole earth^&#42;3^ he will be called.

[NOTE]
====
. “Grand Maker.” Lit., “Makers,” pl. to denote grandeur or excellence.
. “Husbandly owner,” pl. in M to denote grandeur or excellence in agreement with “Grand Maker.”
. “The God of the whole earth.” Heb., ʼElo·hehʹ khol-ha·ʼaʹrets.
====

[[v23-54-6]]

^<<v23-54-6,6>>^ For Jehovah called you as if you were a wife^&#42;1^ left entirely and hurt in spirit, and as a wife of the time of youth who was then rejected,” your God has said.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “as if you were a woman.” Heb., kheʼish·shahʹ.
====

[[v23-54-7]]

^<<v23-54-7,7>>^ “For a little moment^&#42;1^ I left you entirely, but with great mercies I shall collect you together.

[NOTE]
====
. “For a little moment,” MLXXVg; TSy, “With a little anger.”
====

[[v23-54-8]]

^<<v23-54-8,8>>^ With a flood of indignation I concealed my face from you for but a moment, but with loving-kindness^&#42;1^ to time indefinite I will have mercy upon you,” your Repurchaser, Jehovah, has said.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “but with loyal love.” Heb., u·vecheʹsedh.
====

[[v23-54-9]]

^<<v23-54-9,9>>^ “This is just as the days of Noah to me. Just as I have sworn that the waters of Noah shall no more pass over the earth, so I have sworn that I will not become indignant toward you nor rebuke you.

[[v23-54-10]]

^<<v23-54-10,10>>^ For the mountains themselves may be removed, and the very hills may stagger, but my loving-kindness itself will not be removed from you, nor will my covenant of peace itself stagger,” Jehovah, the One having mercy upon you, has said.

[[v23-54-11]]

^<<v23-54-11,11>>^ “O woman afflicted, tempest-tossed, uncomforted, here I am laying with hard mortar your stones, and I will lay your foundation with sapphires.

[[v23-54-12]]

^<<v23-54-12,12>>^ And I will make your battlements of rubies, and your gates of fiery glowing stones, and all your boundaries of delightsome stones.

[[v23-54-13]]

^<<v23-54-13,13>>^ And all your sons will be persons taught^&#42;1^ by Jehovah, and the peace of your sons will be abundant.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “be disciples.” Compare 8:16 ftn, “Disciples.”
====

[[v23-54-14]]

^<<v23-54-14,14>>^ You will prove to be firmly established in righteousness itself. You will be far away^&#42;1^ from oppression—for you will fear none—and from anything terrifying, for it will not come near you.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “Be far away!” Imperative.
====

[[v23-54-15]]

^<<v23-54-15,15>>^ If anyone should at all make an attack, it will not be at my orders. Whoever is making an attack upon you will fall even on account of you.”

[[v23-54-16]]

^<<v23-54-16,16>>^ “Look! I myself have created the craftsman, the one blowing upon the fire of charcoal and bringing forth a weapon as his workmanship. I myself, too, have created the ruinous man for wrecking work.

[[v23-54-17]]

^<<v23-54-17,17>>^ Any weapon whatever that will be formed against you will have no success, and any tongue at all that will rise up against you in the judgment you will condemn.^&#42;1^ This is the hereditary possession of the servants of Jehovah, and their righteousness is from me,” is the utterance of Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “you will pronounce wicked (guilty).”
====
