[[v43-14-0]]
=== According to John, 14
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v43-14-1]]

^<<v43-14-1,1>>^ “Do not let YOUR hearts be troubled. Exercise faith in God, exercise faith also in me.

[[v43-14-2]]

^<<v43-14-2,2>>^ In the house of my Father there are many abodes. Otherwise, I would have told YOU, because I am going my way to prepare a place for YOU.

[[v43-14-3]]

^<<v43-14-3,3>>^ Also, if I go my way and prepare a place for YOU, I am coming again and will receive YOU home to myself, that where I am YOU also may be.

[[v43-14-4]]

^<<v43-14-4,4>>^ And where I am going YOU know the way.”

[[v43-14-5]]

^<<v43-14-5,5>>^ Thomas said to him: “Lord,^&#42;1^ we do not know where you are going. How do we know the way?”

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “Master.”
====

[[v43-14-6]]

^<<v43-14-6,6>>^ Jesus said to him: “I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me.

[[v43-14-7]]

^<<v43-14-7,7>>^ If YOU men had known me, YOU would have known my Father also; from this moment on YOU know him and have seen him.”

[[v43-14-8]]

^<<v43-14-8,8>>^ Philip said to him: “Lord, show us the Father, and it is enough for us.”

[[v43-14-9]]

^<<v43-14-9,9>>^ Jesus said to him: “Have I been with YOU men so long a time, and yet, Philip, you have not come to know me? He that has seen me has seen the Father &#91;also&#93;. How is it you say, ‘Show us the Father’?

[[v43-14-10]]

^<<v43-14-10,10>>^ Do you not believe that I am in union with the Father and the Father is in union with me? The things I say to YOU men I do not speak of my own originality;^&#42;1^ but the Father who remains in union with me is doing his works.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “from myself.”
====

[[v43-14-11]]

^<<v43-14-11,11>>^ Believe me that I am in union with the Father and the Father is in union with me; otherwise, believe on account of the works themselves.

[[v43-14-12]]

^<<v43-14-12,12>>^ Most truly I say to YOU, He that exercises faith in me, that one also will do the works that I do; and he will do works greater than these, because I am going my way to the Father.

[[v43-14-13]]

^<<v43-14-13,13>>^ Also, whatever it is that YOU ask in my name, I will do this, in order that the Father may be glorified in connection with the Son.

[[v43-14-14]]

^<<v43-14-14,14>>^ If YOU ask^&#42;1^ anything in my name, I will do it.

[NOTE]
====
. “Ask,” ADIt and in agreement with 15:16 and 16:23; P66אBWVgSyh,p, “ask me.”
====

[[v43-14-15]]

^<<v43-14-15,15>>^ “If YOU love me, YOU will observe my commandments;

[[v43-14-16]]

^<<v43-14-16,16>>^ and I will request the Father and he will give YOU another helper^&#42;1^ to be with YOU forever,

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “another paraclete (comforter).” Gr., alʹlon pa·raʹkle·ton, masc.
====

[[v43-14-17]]

^<<v43-14-17,17>>^ the spirit^&#42;1^ of the truth, which the world cannot receive, because it neither beholds it nor knows it. YOU know it,^&#42;2^ because it remains with YOU and is in YOU.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the active force.” Gr., to pneuʹma, neuter. See Ge 1:2 ftn, “Force.”
. “Beholds it . . . . You know it.” “It” (Gr., au·toʹ, neuter) refers to “the spirit” (to pneuʹma, neuter).
====

[[v43-14-18]]

^<<v43-14-18,18>>^ I shall not leave YOU bereaved.^&#42;1^ I am coming to YOU.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “orphans.” Gr., or·pha·nousʹ; Lat., orʹfa·nos.
====

[[v43-14-19]]

^<<v43-14-19,19>>^ A little longer and the world will behold me no more, but YOU will behold me, because I live and YOU will live.

[[v43-14-20]]

^<<v43-14-20,20>>^ In that day YOU will know that I am in union with my Father and YOU are in union with me and I am in union with YOU.

[[v43-14-21]]

^<<v43-14-21,21>>^ He that has my commandments and observes them, that one is he who loves me. In turn he that loves me will be loved by my Father, and I will love him and will plainly show myself to him.”

[[v43-14-22]]

^<<v43-14-22,22>>^ Judas, not Is·carʹi·ot, said to him: “Lord, what has happened that you intend to show yourself plainly to us and not to the world?”

[[v43-14-23]]

^<<v43-14-23,23>>^ In answer Jesus said to him: “If anyone loves me, he will observe my word, and my Father will love him, and we shall come to him and make our abode with him.

[[v43-14-24]]

^<<v43-14-24,24>>^ He that does not love me does not observe my words; and the word that YOU are hearing is not mine, but belongs to the Father who sent me.

[[v43-14-25]]

^<<v43-14-25,25>>^ “While remaining with YOU I have spoken these things to YOU.

[[v43-14-26]]

^<<v43-14-26,26>>^ But the helper,^&#42;1^ the holy spirit, which the Father will send in my name, that one^&#42;2^ will teach YOU all things and bring back to YOUR minds all the things I told YOU.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the paraclete (comforter).” Gr., ho . . . pa·raʹkle·tos, masc.
. “That one,” masc., referring to the “helper,” masc.
====

[[v43-14-27]]

^<<v43-14-27,27>>^ I leave YOU peace, I give YOU my peace. I do not give it to YOU the way that the world gives it. Do not let YOUR hearts be troubled nor let them shrink for fear.

[[v43-14-28]]

^<<v43-14-28,28>>^ YOU heard that I said to YOU, I am going away and I am coming &#91;back&#93; to YOU. If YOU loved me, YOU would rejoice that I am going my way to the Father, because the Father is greater than I am.

[[v43-14-29]]

^<<v43-14-29,29>>^ So now I have told YOU before it occurs, in order that, when it does occur, YOU may believe.

[[v43-14-30]]

^<<v43-14-30,30>>^ I shall not speak much^&#42;1^ with YOU anymore, for the ruler of the world is coming. And he has no hold on me,

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “many (things).”
====

[[v43-14-31]]

^<<v43-14-31,31>>^ but, in order for the world to know that I love the Father, even as the Father has given me commandment &#91;to do&#93;, so I am doing. Get up, let us go from here.
