[[v54-2-0]]
=== The First to Timothy, 2
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v54-2-1]]

^<<v54-2-1,1>>^ I therefore exhort, first of all, that supplications, prayers, intercessions, offerings of thanks, be made concerning^&#42;1^ all sorts of men,

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “in behalf of.”
====

[[v54-2-2]]

^<<v54-2-2,2>>^ concerning kings and all those who are in high station; in order that we may go on leading a calm and quiet life with full godly devotion^&#42;1^ and seriousness.

[NOTE]
====
. “Godly devotion,” אAVg; J7,​8, “fear of Jehovah.”
====

[[v54-2-3]]

^<<v54-2-3,3>>^ This is fine and acceptable in the sight of our Savior, God,

[[v54-2-4]]

^<<v54-2-4,4>>^ whose will is that all sorts of men should be saved and come to an accurate knowledge of truth.

[[v54-2-5]]

^<<v54-2-5,5>>^ For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, a man, Christ Jesus,

[[v54-2-6]]

^<<v54-2-6,6>>^ who gave himself a corresponding ransom^&#42;1^ for all^&#42;2^—&#91;this is&#93; what is to be witnessed to at its own particular times.

[NOTE]
====
. “Corresponding ransom.” Gr., an·tiʹly·tron (from an·tiʹ, “against; in correspondence to; in place of,” and lyʹtron, “ransom [price paid]”). Compare Mt 20:28 ftn, “Ransom.”
. Or, “all sorts of people.”
====

[[v54-2-7]]

^<<v54-2-7,7>>^ For the purpose of this witness I was appointed a preacher and an apostle—I am telling the truth, I am not lying—a teacher of nations in the matter of faith and truth.

[[v54-2-8]]

^<<v54-2-8,8>>^ Therefore I desire that in every place the men carry on prayer, lifting up loyal hands, apart from wrath and debates.

[[v54-2-9]]

^<<v54-2-9,9>>^ Likewise I desire the women to adorn themselves in well-arranged dress, with modesty and soundness of mind, not with styles of hair braiding and gold or pearls or very expensive garb,

[[v54-2-10]]

^<<v54-2-10,10>>^ but in the way that befits women professing to reverence God,^&#42;1^ namely, through good works.

[NOTE]
====
. “To reverence God,” אA; J7,​8, “fear of Jehovah.”
====

[[v54-2-11]]

^<<v54-2-11,11>>^ Let a woman learn in silence^&#42;1^ with full submissiveness.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “quietness.”
====

[[v54-2-12]]

^<<v54-2-12,12>>^ I do not permit a woman to teach, or to exercise authority over a man, but to be in silence.

[[v54-2-13]]

^<<v54-2-13,13>>^ For Adam was formed first, then Eve.

[[v54-2-14]]

^<<v54-2-14,14>>^ Also, Adam was not deceived, but the woman was thoroughly deceived and came to be in transgression.

[[v54-2-15]]

^<<v54-2-15,15>>^ However, she will be kept safe^&#42;1^ through childbearing, provided they continue in faith and love and sanctification along with soundness of mind.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “be saved.”
====
