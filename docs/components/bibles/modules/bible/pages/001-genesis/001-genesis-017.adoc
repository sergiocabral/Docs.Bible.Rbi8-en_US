[[v1-17-0]]
=== Genesis, 17
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v1-17-1]]

^<<v1-17-1,1>>^ When Aʹbram got to be ninety-nine years old, then Jehovah appeared to Aʹbram and said to him: “I am God Almighty.^&#42;1^ Walk before me and prove yourself faultless.

[NOTE]
====
. “God Almighty.” Heb., ʼEl Shad·daiʹ.
====

[[v1-17-2]]

^<<v1-17-2,2>>^ And I will give my covenant between me and you, that I may multiply you very, very much.”

[[v1-17-3]]

^<<v1-17-3,3>>^ At this Aʹbram fell upon his face, and God^&#42;1^ continued to speak with him, saying:

[NOTE]
====
. Heb., ʼElo·himʹ, pl., without the definite article.
====

[[v1-17-4]]

^<<v1-17-4,4>>^ “As for me, look! my covenant is with you, and you will certainly become a father of a crowd of nations.

[[v1-17-5]]

^<<v1-17-5,5>>^ And your name will not be called Aʹbram^&#42;1^ anymore, and your name must become Abraham,^&#42;2^ because a father of a crowd of nations I will make you.

[NOTE]
====
. Meaning “Father of Exaltation.” Heb., ʼAv·ramʹ.
. Meaning “Father of a Multitude.” Heb., ʼAv·ra·hamʹ.
====

[[v1-17-6]]

^<<v1-17-6,6>>^ And I will make you very, very fruitful and will make you become nations, and kings will come out of you.

[[v1-17-7]]

^<<v1-17-7,7>>^ “And I will carry out my covenant between me and you and your seed after you according to their generations for a covenant to time indefinite, to prove myself God^&#42;1^ to you and to your seed after you.

[NOTE]
====
. “Myself God.” Lit., “for a God.” Heb., lEʼ·lo·himʹ, pl., without the definite article.
====

[[v1-17-8]]

^<<v1-17-8,8>>^ And I will give to you and to your seed after you the land of your alien residences, even the entire land of Caʹnaan, for a possession to time indefinite; and I will prove myself God to them.”

[[v1-17-9]]

^<<v1-17-9,9>>^ And God said further to Abraham: “As for you, you are to keep my covenant, you and your seed after you according to their generations.

[[v1-17-10]]

^<<v1-17-10,10>>^ This is my covenant that YOU men will keep, between me and YOU men, even your seed after you: Every male of YOURS must get circumcised.

[[v1-17-11]]

^<<v1-17-11,11>>^ And YOU must get circumcised in the flesh of YOUR foreskins, and it must serve as a sign of the covenant between me and YOU.

[[v1-17-12]]

^<<v1-17-12,12>>^ And every male of YOURS eight days old must be circumcised, according to your generations, anyone born in the house^&#42;1^ and anyone purchased with money^&#42;2^ from any foreigner who is not from your seed.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “any son of a slave.”
. Lit., “silver.” Heb., keʹseph.
====

[[v1-17-13]]

^<<v1-17-13,13>>^ Every man born in your house and every man purchased with money of yours must without fail get circumcised; and my covenant in the flesh of YOU men must serve as a covenant to time indefinite.

[[v1-17-14]]

^<<v1-17-14,14>>^ And an uncircumcised male who will not get the flesh of his foreskin circumcised, even that soul must be cut off from his people. He has broken my covenant.”

[[v1-17-15]]

^<<v1-17-15,15>>^ And God went on to say to Abraham: “As for Sarʹai your wife, you must not call her name Sarʹai,^&#42;1^ because Sarah^&#42;2^ is her name.

[NOTE]
====
. Meaning “Contentious.” Heb., Sa·raiʹ.
. Meaning “Princess.” Heb., Sa·rahʹ.
====

[[v1-17-16]]

^<<v1-17-16,16>>^ And I will bless her and also give you a son from her; and I will bless her and she shall become nations; kings of peoples will come from her.”

[[v1-17-17]]

^<<v1-17-17,17>>^ At this Abraham fell upon his face and began to laugh and to say in his heart: “Will a man a hundred years old have a child born, and will Sarah, yes, will a woman ninety years old give birth?”

[[v1-17-18]]

^<<v1-17-18,18>>^ After that Abraham said to the &#91;true&#93; God:^&#42;1^ “O that Ishʹma·el might live before you!”

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1F.
====

[[v1-17-19]]

^<<v1-17-19,19>>^ To this God said: “Sarah your wife is indeed bearing you a son, and you must call his name Isaac.^&#42;1^ And I will establish my covenant with him for a covenant to time indefinite to his seed after him.

[NOTE]
====
. Meaning “Laughter.” Heb., Yits·chaqʹ; an example of onomatopoeia, where the sound suggests the sense.
====

[[v1-17-20]]

^<<v1-17-20,20>>^ But as regards Ishʹma·el I have heard you. Look! I will bless him and will make him fruitful and will multiply him very, very much. He will certainly produce twelve chieftains,^&#42;1^ and I will make him become a great nation.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “nasis.” Heb., nesi·ʼimʹ; Vg, “dukes; leaders”; LXX, “nations.”
====

[[v1-17-21]]

^<<v1-17-21,21>>^ However, my covenant I shall establish with Isaac, whom Sarah will bear to you at this appointed time next year.”

[[v1-17-22]]

^<<v1-17-22,22>>^ With that God finished speaking with him and went up from Abraham.

[[v1-17-23]]

^<<v1-17-23,23>>^ Abraham then proceeded to take Ishʹma·el his son and all the men born in his house and everyone purchased with money of his, every male among the men of the household of Abraham, and he went to circumcising the flesh of their foreskins in this very day, just as God had spoken with him.

[[v1-17-24]]

^<<v1-17-24,24>>^ And Abraham was ninety-nine years old when he had the flesh of his foreskin circumcised.

[[v1-17-25]]

^<<v1-17-25,25>>^ And Ishʹma·el his son was thirteen years old when he had the flesh of his foreskin circumcised.

[[v1-17-26]]

^<<v1-17-26,26>>^ In this very day Abraham got circumcised, and also Ishʹma·el his son.

[[v1-17-27]]

^<<v1-17-27,27>>^ And all the men of his household, anyone born in the house and anyone purchased with money from a foreigner, got circumcised with him.
