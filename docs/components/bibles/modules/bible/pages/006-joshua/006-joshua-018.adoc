[[v6-18-0]]
=== Joshua, 18
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v6-18-1]]

^<<v6-18-1,1>>^ Then all the assembly of the sons of Israel were congregated at Shiʹloh, and they proceeded to locate the tent of meeting there, as the land was now subdued before them.

[[v6-18-2]]

^<<v6-18-2,2>>^ But there were still left among the sons of Israel those whose inheritance they had not apportioned out, namely, seven tribes.

[[v6-18-3]]

^<<v6-18-3,3>>^ So Joshua said to the sons of Israel: “How long are YOU going to be delinquent about going in to take possession of the land that Jehovah the God of YOUR forefathers has given YOU?

[[v6-18-4]]

^<<v6-18-4,4>>^ Furnish for yourselves three men of each tribe and let me send them out, that they may get up and walk about in the land and map it out in accord with their inheritance, and let them come to me.

[[v6-18-5]]

^<<v6-18-5,5>>^ And they must apportion it among themselves into seven shares. Judah will keep standing on his territory to the south, and the house of Joseph will keep standing on their territory to the north.

[[v6-18-6]]

^<<v6-18-6,6>>^ As for YOU people, YOU will map out the land into seven shares, and YOU must bring them here to me, and I must cast lots here for YOU before Jehovah our God.

[[v6-18-7]]

^<<v6-18-7,7>>^ For the Levites have no share in among YOU, because the priesthood of Jehovah is their inheritance; and Gad and Reuʹben and the half tribe of Ma·nasʹseh have taken their inheritance on the side of the Jordan toward the east, which Moses the servant of Jehovah has given them.”

[[v6-18-8]]

^<<v6-18-8,8>>^ So the men got up that they might go, and Joshua proceeded to command those who were going to map out the land, saying: “Go and walk about in the land and map it out and return to me, and here is where I shall draw lots for YOU before Jehovah in Shiʹloh.”

[[v6-18-9]]

^<<v6-18-9,9>>^ With that the men went and passed through the land and mapped it out by cities in seven shares, in a book. After that they came to Joshua at the camp in Shiʹloh,

[[v6-18-10]]

^<<v6-18-10,10>>^ and Joshua went drawing lots for them in Shiʹloh before Jehovah. Thus Joshua there apportioned the land to the sons of Israel in their shares.

[[v6-18-11]]

^<<v6-18-11,11>>^ Then the lot came up of the tribe of the sons of Benjamin by their families, and the territory of their lot went out between the sons of Judah and the sons of Joseph.

[[v6-18-12]]

^<<v6-18-12,12>>^ And their boundary came to be at the northern corner from the Jordan, and the boundary went up to the slope of Jerʹi·cho on the north and went up on the mountain westward, and its termination proved to be at the wilderness of Beth-aʹven.

[[v6-18-13]]

^<<v6-18-13,13>>^ And the boundary passed over from there to Luz, at the southern slope of Luz, that is to say, Bethʹel; and the boundary went down to Atʹa·roth-adʹdar upon the mountain that is on the south of Lower Beth-hoʹron.

[[v6-18-14]]

^<<v6-18-14,14>>^ And the boundary was marked out and went around at the western side to the south from the mountain that faces Beth-hoʹron to the south; and its termination proved to be at Kirʹi·ath-baʹal, that is to say, Kirʹi·ath-jeʹa·rim, a city of the sons of Judah. This is the western side.

[[v6-18-15]]

^<<v6-18-15,15>>^ And the side to the south was from the extremity of Kirʹi·ath-jeʹa·rim, and the boundary went out westward and went out to the spring of the waters of Neph·toʹah.

[[v6-18-16]]

^<<v6-18-16,16>>^ And the boundary went down to the extremity of the mountain that faces the valley of the son of Hinʹnom, which is in the low plain of Rephʹa·im to the north, and it went down to the valley of Hinʹnom,^&#42;1^ to the slope of the Jebʹu·site on the south, and went down to En-roʹgel.

[NOTE]
====
. “Valley of Hinnom.” Heb., geh Hin·nomʹ; Gr., Gaiʹen·na; Lat., Ge·henʹnom. From this comes the English word “Gehenna.” See App 4C.
====

[[v6-18-17]]

^<<v6-18-17,17>>^ And it was marked out northward and went out to En-sheʹmesh and went out to Gel·iʹloth, which is in front of the ascent of A·dumʹmim; and it went down to the stone of Boʹhan the son of Reuʹben.

[[v6-18-18]]

^<<v6-18-18,18>>^ And it passed over to the northern slope in front of the Arʹa·bah and went down to the Arʹa·bah.

[[v6-18-19]]

^<<v6-18-19,19>>^ And the boundary passed over to the northern slope of Beth-hogʹlah, and the termination of it (the border) proved to be at the northern bay of the Salt Sea at the southern end of the Jordan. This was the southern boundary.

[[v6-18-20]]

^<<v6-18-20,20>>^ And the Jordan served as its boundary on the eastern side. This was the inheritance of the sons of Benjamin by their families, by its boundaries all around.

[[v6-18-21]]

^<<v6-18-21,21>>^ And the cities of the tribe of the sons of Benjamin by their families proved to be Jerʹi·cho and Beth-hogʹlah and Eʹmek-keʹziz,

[[v6-18-22]]

^<<v6-18-22,22>>^ and Beth-arʹa·bah and Zem·a·raʹim and Bethʹel,

[[v6-18-23]]

^<<v6-18-23,23>>^ and Avʹvim and Paʹrah and Ophʹrah,

[[v6-18-24]]

^<<v6-18-24,24>>^ and Cheʹphar-amʹmo·ni and Ophʹni and Geʹba; twelve cities and their settlements.

[[v6-18-25]]

^<<v6-18-25,25>>^ Gibʹe·on and Raʹmah and Be·erʹoth,

[[v6-18-26]]

^<<v6-18-26,26>>^ and Mizʹpeh and Che·phiʹrah and Moʹzah,

[[v6-18-27]]

^<<v6-18-27,27>>^ and Reʹkem and Irʹpe·el and Tarʹa·lah,

[[v6-18-28]]

^<<v6-18-28,28>>^ and Zeʹlah, Ha-eʹleph and Je·buʹsi,^&#42;1^ that is to say, Jerusalem, Gibʹe·ah and Kirʹi·ath; fourteen cities and their settlements.
This was the inheritance of the sons of Benjamin by their families.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “the Jebusite,” M; LXXSyVg, “Jebus.”
====
