[[v46-3-0]]
=== The First to the Corinthians, 3
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v46-3-1]]

^<<v46-3-1,1>>^ And so, brothers, I was not able to speak to YOU as to spiritual men, but as to fleshly men, as to babes in Christ.

[[v46-3-2]]

^<<v46-3-2,2>>^ I fed YOU milk, not something to eat, for YOU were not yet strong enough. In fact, neither are YOU strong enough now,

[[v46-3-3]]

^<<v46-3-3,3>>^ for YOU are yet fleshly. For whereas there are jealousy and strife among YOU, are YOU not fleshly and are YOU not walking as men do?

[[v46-3-4]]

^<<v46-3-4,4>>^ For when one says: “I belong to Paul,” but another says: “I to A·polʹlos,” are YOU not simply men?

[[v46-3-5]]

^<<v46-3-5,5>>^ What, then, is A·polʹlos? Yes, what is Paul? Ministers^&#42;1^ through whom YOU became believers, even as the Lord granted each one.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “Servants.” Gr., di·aʹko·noi; Lat., mi·niʹstri (from miʹnus, “less”); J18,​22(Heb.), mesha·rethimʹ, “attendants.”
====

[[v46-3-6]]

^<<v46-3-6,6>>^ I planted, A·polʹlos watered, but God kept making &#91;it&#93; grow;

[[v46-3-7]]

^<<v46-3-7,7>>^ so that neither is he that plants anything nor is he that waters, but God who makes &#91;it&#93; grow.

[[v46-3-8]]

^<<v46-3-8,8>>^ Now he that plants and he that waters are one,^&#42;1^ but each &#91;person&#93; will receive his own reward according to his own labor.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “one (thing).” Gr., hen, neuter, showing a oneness in cooperation. See Joh 10:30 ftn.
====

[[v46-3-9]]

^<<v46-3-9,9>>^ For we are God’s fellow workers. YOU people are God’s field under cultivation, God’s building.

[[v46-3-10]]

^<<v46-3-10,10>>^ According to the undeserved kindness of God that was given to me, as a wise director of works I laid a foundation, but someone else is building on it. But let each one keep watching how he is building on it.

[[v46-3-11]]

^<<v46-3-11,11>>^ For no man can lay any other foundation than what is laid, which is Jesus Christ.

[[v46-3-12]]

^<<v46-3-12,12>>^ Now if anyone builds on the foundation gold, silver, precious stones, wood materials, hay, stubble,

[[v46-3-13]]

^<<v46-3-13,13>>^ each one’s work will become manifest, for the day will show it up, because it will be revealed by means of fire; and the fire itself will prove what sort of work each one’s is.

[[v46-3-14]]

^<<v46-3-14,14>>^ If anyone’s work that he has built on it remains, he will receive a reward;

[[v46-3-15]]

^<<v46-3-15,15>>^ if anyone’s work is burned up, he will suffer loss, but he himself will be saved; yet, if so, &#91;it will be&#93; as through fire.

[[v46-3-16]]

^<<v46-3-16,16>>^ Do YOU not know that YOU people are God’s temple,^&#42;1^ and that the spirit of God dwells in YOU?

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “divine habitation (dwelling).” Gr., na·osʹ; Lat., temʹplum; J17,​22(Heb.), heh·khalʹ, “palace (temple) of.”
====

[[v46-3-17]]

^<<v46-3-17,17>>^ If anyone destroys the temple of God, God will destroy him; for the temple of God is holy, which &#91;temple&#93; YOU people are.

[[v46-3-18]]

^<<v46-3-18,18>>^ Let no one be seducing himself: If anyone among YOU thinks he is wise in this system of things,^&#42;1^ let him become a fool, that he may become wise.

[NOTE]
====
. See 1:20 ftn.
====

[[v46-3-19]]

^<<v46-3-19,19>>^ For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God; for it is written: “He catches the wise in their own cunning.”

[[v46-3-20]]

^<<v46-3-20,20>>^ And again: “Jehovah^&#42;1^ knows that the reasonings of the wise men are futile.”

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1D.
====

[[v46-3-21]]

^<<v46-3-21,21>>^ Hence let no one be boasting in men; for all things belong to YOU,

[[v46-3-22]]

^<<v46-3-22,22>>^ whether Paul or A·polʹlos or Ceʹphas or the world or life or death or things now here or things to come, all things belong to YOU;

[[v46-3-23]]

^<<v46-3-23,23>>^ in turn YOU belong to Christ; Christ, in turn, belongs to God.
