[[v52-4-0]]
=== The First to the Thessalonians, 4
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v52-4-1]]

^<<v52-4-1,1>>^ Finally, brothers, we request YOU and exhort YOU by the Lord Jesus, just as YOU received &#91;the instruction&#93; from us on how YOU ought to walk and please God, just as YOU are in fact walking, that YOU would keep on doing it more fully.

[[v52-4-2]]

^<<v52-4-2,2>>^ For YOU know the orders we gave YOU through the Lord Jesus.

[[v52-4-3]]

^<<v52-4-3,3>>^ For this is what God wills, the sanctifying^&#42;1^ of YOU, that YOU abstain from fornication;^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “the holding sacred (treating as holy).” Gr., ho ha·gi·a·smosʹ; Lat., sanc·ti·fi·caʹti·o.
. See App 5A.
====

[[v52-4-4]]

^<<v52-4-4,4>>^ that each one of YOU should know how to get possession of his own vessel in sanctification and honor,

[[v52-4-5]]

^<<v52-4-5,5>>^ not in covetous sexual appetite^&#42;1^ such as also those nations have which do not know God;

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “not in passion of desire.”
====

[[v52-4-6]]

^<<v52-4-6,6>>^ that no one go to the point of harming and encroach upon the rights of his brother in this matter, because Jehovah^&#42;1^ is one who exacts punishment for all these things, just as we told YOU beforehand and also gave YOU a thorough witness.

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1D.
====

[[v52-4-7]]

^<<v52-4-7,7>>^ For God called us, not with allowance for uncleanness,^&#42;1^ but in connection with sanctification.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “filthiness; depravity; lewdness.” Gr., a·ka·thar·siʹai, dative, sing.; J22(Heb.), letum·ʼahʹ, “for uncleanness.”
====

[[v52-4-8]]

^<<v52-4-8,8>>^ So, then, the man that shows disregard is disregarding, not man, but God, who puts his holy spirit in YOU.

[[v52-4-9]]

^<<v52-4-9,9>>^ However, with reference to brotherly love, YOU do not need us to be writing YOU, for YOU yourselves are taught by God^&#42;1^ to love one another;

[NOTE]
====
. “God,” אABVg; J17, “Jehovah.”
====

[[v52-4-10]]

^<<v52-4-10,10>>^ and, in fact, YOU are doing it to all the brothers in all of Mac·e·doʹni·a. But we exhort YOU, brothers, to go on doing it in fuller measure,

[[v52-4-11]]

^<<v52-4-11,11>>^ and to make it YOUR aim to live quietly and to mind YOUR own business and work with YOUR hands, just as we ordered YOU;

[[v52-4-12]]

^<<v52-4-12,12>>^ so that YOU may be walking decently as regards people outside and not be needing anything.

[[v52-4-13]]

^<<v52-4-13,13>>^ Moreover, brothers, we do not want YOU to be ignorant concerning those who are sleeping &#91;in death&#93;; that YOU may not sorrow just as the rest also do who have no hope.

[[v52-4-14]]

^<<v52-4-14,14>>^ For if our faith is that Jesus died and rose again, so, too, those who have fallen asleep &#91;in death&#93; through Jesus God will bring with him.

[[v52-4-15]]

^<<v52-4-15,15>>^ For this is what we tell YOU by Jehovah’s^&#42;1^ word, that we the living who survive to the presence of the Lord^&#42;2^ shall in no way precede those who have fallen asleep &#91;in death&#93;;

[NOTE]
====
. See App 1D.
. “The Lord,” אAVg; B, “Jesus.”
====

[[v52-4-16]]

^<<v52-4-16,16>>^ because the Lord^&#42;1^ himself will descend from heaven with a commanding call, with an archangel’s voice and with God’s trumpet, and those who are dead in union with Christ will rise first.

[NOTE]
====
. “The Lord,” אABVg; J7,​8,​13,​14, “Jehovah.”
====

[[v52-4-17]]

^<<v52-4-17,17>>^ Afterward we the living who are surviving^&#42;1^ will, together^&#42;2^ with them, be caught away in clouds to meet the Lord^&#42;3^ in the air; and thus we shall always be with &#91;the&#93; Lord.^&#42;4^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “the (ones) being left around.” See App 5D.
. Or, “at the same time.”
. “The Lord,” אABVg; J7,​8, “Jehovah.”
. “Lord,” אABVg; J7,​8,​13,​14,​24, “Jehovah.”
====

[[v52-4-18]]

^<<v52-4-18,18>>^ Consequently keep comforting one another with these words.
