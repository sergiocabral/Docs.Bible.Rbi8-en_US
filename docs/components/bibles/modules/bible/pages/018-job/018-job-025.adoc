[[v18-25-0]]
=== Job, 25
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v18-25-1]]

^<<v18-25-1,1>>^ And Bilʹdad the Shuʹhite proceeded to answer and say:

[[v18-25-2]]

^<<v18-25-2,2>>^ “Rulership and dreadfulness are with him;
He is making peace on his heights.

[[v18-25-3]]

^<<v18-25-3,3>>^ Is there any number to his troops?
And upon whom does his light not rise?

[[v18-25-4]]

^<<v18-25-4,4>>^ So how can mortal man^&#42;1^ be in the right before God,^&#42;2^
Or how can one born of a woman be clean?

[NOTE]
====
. “Mortal man.” Heb., ʼenohshʹ.
. “God.” Heb., ʼEl.
====

[[v18-25-5]]

^<<v18-25-5,5>>^ Look! There is even the moon, and it is not bright;
And the stars themselves have not proved clean in his eyes.

[[v18-25-6]]

^<<v18-25-6,6>>^ How much less so mortal man, who is a maggot,
And a son of man,^&#42;1^ who is a worm!”

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “earthling man.” Heb., ʼa·dhamʹ.
====
