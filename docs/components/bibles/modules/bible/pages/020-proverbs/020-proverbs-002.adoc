[[v20-2-0]]
=== Proverbs, 2
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v20-2-1]]

^<<v20-2-1,1>>^ My son, if you will receive my sayings and treasure up my own commandments with yourself,

[[v20-2-2]]

^<<v20-2-2,2>>^ so as to pay attention to wisdom with your ear, that you may incline your heart to discernment;

[[v20-2-3]]

^<<v20-2-3,3>>^ if, moreover, you call out for understanding itself and you give forth your voice for discernment itself,

[[v20-2-4]]

^<<v20-2-4,4>>^ if you keep seeking for it as for silver, and as for hid treasures you keep searching for it,

[[v20-2-5]]

^<<v20-2-5,5>>^ in that case you will understand the fear of Jehovah, and you will find the very knowledge of God.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “God.” Heb., ʼElo·himʹ.
====

[[v20-2-6]]

^<<v20-2-6,6>>^ For Jehovah himself gives wisdom; out of his mouth there are knowledge and discernment.

[[v20-2-7]]

^<<v20-2-7,7>>^ And for the upright ones he will treasure up practical wisdom; for those walking in integrity he is a shield,

[[v20-2-8]]

^<<v20-2-8,8>>^ by observing the paths of judgment, and he will guard the very way of his loyal ones.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “his men of loving-kindness,” Mmargin (TLXXSyVg are similar); M, “his man of loving-kindness.”
====

[[v20-2-9]]

^<<v20-2-9,9>>^ In that case you will understand righteousness and judgment and uprightness, the entire course of what is good.

[[v20-2-10]]

^<<v20-2-10,10>>^ When wisdom enters into your heart and knowledge itself becomes pleasant to your very soul,

[[v20-2-11]]

^<<v20-2-11,11>>^ thinking ability itself will keep guard over you, discernment itself will safeguard you,

[[v20-2-12]]

^<<v20-2-12,12>>^ to deliver you from the bad way, from the man speaking perverse things,

[[v20-2-13]]

^<<v20-2-13,13>>^ from those leaving the paths of uprightness to walk in the ways of darkness,

[[v20-2-14]]

^<<v20-2-14,14>>^ from those who are rejoicing in doing bad, who are joyful in the perverse things of badness;

[[v20-2-15]]

^<<v20-2-15,15>>^ those whose paths are crooked and who are devious in their general course;

[[v20-2-16]]

^<<v20-2-16,16>>^ to deliver you from the strange woman, from the foreign woman who has made her own sayings smooth,

[[v20-2-17]]

^<<v20-2-17,17>>^ who is leaving the confidential friend of her youth and who has forgotten the very covenant of her God.

[[v20-2-18]]

^<<v20-2-18,18>>^ For down to death her house does sink and down to those impotent in death^&#42;1^ her tracks.

[NOTE]
====
. “Those impotent in death.” Heb., repha·ʼimʹ; T(Aram.), gib·bo·raiʹia, “the giants”; LXX, “Hades [Gr., haiʹdei] with the earthborn ones”; Vgc(Lat.), inʹfe·ros.
====

[[v20-2-19]]

^<<v20-2-19,19>>^ None of those having relations with her will come back, nor will they regain the paths of those living.

[[v20-2-20]]

^<<v20-2-20,20>>^ The purpose is that you may walk in the way of good people and that the paths of the righteous ones you may keep.

[[v20-2-21]]

^<<v20-2-21,21>>^ For the upright are the ones that will reside in the earth, and the blameless are the ones that will be left over in it.

[[v20-2-22]]

^<<v20-2-22,22>>^ As regards the wicked, they will be cut off from the very earth; and as for the treacherous, they will be torn away from it.
