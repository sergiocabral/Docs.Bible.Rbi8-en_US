[[v20-22-0]]
=== Proverbs, 22
// Rbi8 1984, Rbi8 - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures—With References

[[v20-22-1]]

^<<v20-22-1,1>>^ A name^&#42;1^ is to be chosen rather than abundant riches; favor is better than even silver and gold.

[NOTE]
====
. “A name,” M(Heb., shem)Sy; TLXXVg, “A good name.”
====

[[v20-22-2]]

^<<v20-22-2,2>>^ The rich one and the one of little means have met each other. The Maker of them all is Jehovah.

[[v20-22-3]]

^<<v20-22-3,3>>^ Shrewd is the one that has seen the calamity and proceeds to conceal himself, but the inexperienced have passed along and must suffer the penalty.

[[v20-22-4]]

^<<v20-22-4,4>>^ The result of humility &#91;and&#93; the fear of Jehovah is riches and glory and life.

[[v20-22-5]]

^<<v20-22-5,5>>^ Thorns &#91;and&#93; traps are in the way of the crooked one; he that is guarding his soul keeps far away from them.

[[v20-22-6]]

^<<v20-22-6,6>>^ Train up a boy according to^&#42;1^ the way for him; even when he grows old he will not turn aside from it.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “at the mouth (opening) of.”
====

[[v20-22-7]]

^<<v20-22-7,7>>^ The rich is the one that rules over those of little means, and the borrower is servant to the man doing the lending.

[[v20-22-8]]

^<<v20-22-8,8>>^ He that is sowing unrighteousness will reap what is hurtful, but the very rod of his fury will come to its end.

[[v20-22-9]]

^<<v20-22-9,9>>^ He that is kindly in eye will be blessed, for he has given of his food to the lowly one.

[[v20-22-10]]

^<<v20-22-10,10>>^ Drive away the ridiculer, that contention may go out and that legal contest and dishonor may cease.

[[v20-22-11]]

^<<v20-22-11,11>>^ The one loving purity of heart^&#42;1^—for the charm of his lips the king will be his companion.

[NOTE]
====
. “God loves the purehearted one,” TSy; LXX, “Jehovah loves pure hearts.”
====

[[v20-22-12]]

^<<v20-22-12,12>>^ The eyes of Jehovah himself have safeguarded knowledge, but he subverts the words^&#42;1^ of the treacherous one.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “affairs.”
====

[[v20-22-13]]

^<<v20-22-13,13>>^ The lazy one has said: “There is a lion outside! In the midst of the public squares I shall be murdered!”

[[v20-22-14]]

^<<v20-22-14,14>>^ The mouth of strange women is a deep pit. The one denounced by Jehovah will fall into it.

[[v20-22-15]]

^<<v20-22-15,15>>^ Foolishness is tied up with the heart of a boy; the rod of discipline is what will remove it far from him.

[[v20-22-16]]

^<<v20-22-16,16>>^ He that is defrauding the lowly one to supply many things to himself, he also that is giving to the rich one, is surely destined for want.

[[v20-22-17]]

^<<v20-22-17,17>>^ Incline your ear and hear the words of the wise ones, that you may apply your very heart to my knowledge.

[[v20-22-18]]

^<<v20-22-18,18>>^ For it is pleasant that you should keep them in your belly, that they may be firmly established together upon your lips.

[[v20-22-19]]

^<<v20-22-19,19>>^ For your confidence to come to be in Jehovah himself I have given you knowledge today, even you.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. “Caused you to know your paths today,” by a slight correction of M, in agreement with LXX.
====

[[v20-22-20]]

^<<v20-22-20,20>>^ Have I not written you heretofore^&#42;1^ with counselings and knowledge,

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “in thirty sections (parts).” M is unclear.
====

[[v20-22-21]]

^<<v20-22-21,21>>^ to show you the truthfulness^&#42;1^ of true sayings, so as to return sayings that are the truth—to the one sending you forth?

[NOTE]
====
. “Truthfulness of,” qosht, a word found in Biblical Aram., as in Da 2:47.
====

[[v20-22-22]]

^<<v20-22-22,22>>^ Do not rob the lowly one because he is lowly, and do not crush the afflicted one in the gate.

[[v20-22-23]]

^<<v20-22-23,23>>^ For Jehovah himself will plead their cause, and he will certainly rob of soul^&#42;1^ those robbing them.

[NOTE]
====
. Or, “life.” Heb., naʹphesh; Gr., psy·khenʹ; Lat., aʹni·mam. See App 4A.
====

[[v20-22-24]]

^<<v20-22-24,24>>^ Do not have companionship with anyone given to anger; and with a man having fits of rage you must not enter in,

[[v20-22-25]]

^<<v20-22-25,25>>^ that you may not get familiar with his paths and certainly take a snare for your soul.

[[v20-22-26]]

^<<v20-22-26,26>>^ Do not get to be among those striking hands, among those who go security for loans.

[[v20-22-27]]

^<<v20-22-27,27>>^ If you have nothing to pay, why should he take your bed from under you?

[[v20-22-28]]

^<<v20-22-28,28>>^ Do not move back a boundary of long ago, which your forefathers have made.

[[v20-22-29]]

^<<v20-22-29,29>>^ Have you beheld a man skillful in his work? Before kings is where he will station himself; he will not station himself before commonplace men.
